[QUOTE=Loofiloo;27975526]Is there someplace I can read an explanation of exactly how monster-spawning works? My friends and I built a walled city, and I thought putting torches all over the place would keep monsters from spawning inside, but we are still regularly plagued by creatures at night.[/QUOTE] Mobs will not spawn within 24 blocks from you. They will not spawn further than 80 blocks from you. They need a light level of 7 or below, torches put out light levels of 14 decreasing by 1 every block, so keep torches relatively close. Make sure there's no trees with tops that are unlit, as they can spawn there, too. Mobs cannot spawn on stone slabs or stairs, nor ice or water.
