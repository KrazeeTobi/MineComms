He goes inside and locks the door. He takes all the material he picked up during the day and puts them in their proper chests. He opens the mining chest, put's on his iron armor, and puts four torches, a shovel, and some sticks into his bag. He grabs his sword and heads underground. Left, Left, Right, Right. He always remembers that this is the way to go. Torches mounded on the cold stone walls light up his path. After a short walk, Redmonn finds Mamoto and Wad sleeping in a freshly carved out tunnel. He nudges Mamoto awake. "How much did you two get?" Ask Redmonn. Mamoto leads Redmonn to their progress chest, and Redmonn starts counting. Nine chunks of iron, two chunks of coal. "We are almost dry out of coal. Using this coal, we only have Twenty torches left. We can't melt down this iron." Said Redmonn. "I know...." SSSSSSSSSSSSSSS- "Wha-" [i]BOOM![/i] An explosion blast a hole in the wall left of Redmonn and Mamoto, and also wakes up Wad. Two zombies emerge from the hole. Redmonn hops to his feet, grabs his sword, and sprints towards the zombies. Duck, impale, and avoid the arms. This is Redmonns own self taught fighting style. Redmonn quickly takes the sword out of his stomach. He does a complete spin around the zombie, and uses the force to help slash the zombies head clean off. As Redmonn turns around, the other zombie is mere inches from grabbing him. Startled, Redmonn jumps backwards and lands on his back. As he recollects himself and get's up, Mamoto is sprinting toward the zombie. Mamoto swings the stone pick into the monsters neck. The sheer force of Mamoto's strength not only impales the next completely through, but also sends the zombie down to the ground with incredible force. Redmonn brings his sword down between it's eyes for the killing blow. Their corpses dissapear, leaving behind 2 feathers each. Redmonn lights a torch, grips his sword tight, and prepares to enter the hole the blast left behind.
