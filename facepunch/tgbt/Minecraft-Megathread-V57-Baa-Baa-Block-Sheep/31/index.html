A little idea I had to do with spawn points... I thought it would be a good idea to have beds. Essentially, the player first creates a pillow and a blanket. These can be placed normally next to each other and would resemble a bed, good for putting in rooms in your buildings to make them appear to be furnished, or on their own (pillows on couches etc). They would appear as a very thin block such as how snow fits on other blocks. Pillows could be 2 blocks of wool with a feather between them, the blanket a square of wool blocks, maybe allowing these to be dyed too. With these the player can then craft a bed, by placing them next to each other in the crafting grid, which when placed looks like a bed ready to sleep in (folder blanket corner or something). This would become the players spawn point if they died, and creating a new bed simply unmakes the old one into a decorative blanket and pillow. As this can be put together in the inventory crafting grid it means players can make a camp without needing a crafting table if they are only stopping for the night, and different players on SMP can have their own spawn points. Thoughts?
