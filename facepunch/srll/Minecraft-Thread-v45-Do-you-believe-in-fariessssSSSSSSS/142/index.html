This is a sad story of some fish, and a man. So, with the Halloween update, we were introduced to biomes and fish. Excited by the prospect of snow, I built myself an igloo, complete with a fire moat surrounding it, which used the new netherstone, also included in the update. It was nothing that impressive for an average Minecraft player, but I was proud of it. [URL=http://imgur.com/ZU3re.png][IMG]http://imgur.com/ZU3rel.jpg[/IMG][/URL] My igloo, however, was very lonely. After long days exploring mines, oceans and forests, I would come home to an empty igloo. So, I decided to go and find myself some friends. I waited until nightfall, and viciously attacked every spider I could find. I was eventually able to collect the string required to craft a fishing pole. The next morning, I headed out to my fishing pond, and wrangled a fish. He was a cute little fella, certainly no trophy winner, but I thought that we would be good friends. I also caught a couple fellow fish to keep him company while I was away. [URL=http://imgur.com/j9s9s.png][IMG]http://imgur.com/j9s9sl.jpg[/IMG][/URL] For them I constructed a fish tank of the highest quality. A large glass tank, with ample lighting, a gravel bottom, and pristine water. They rejoiced at the excitement of having their own home, safe from renegade chickens and spiders that they would encounter outside. [URL=http://imgur.com/W8zrD.png][IMG]http://imgur.com/W8zrDl.jpg[/IMG][/URL] We talked, laughed, and shot the breeze all through the night. Life, love, hate, everything was a topic of conversation. We had a lot of fun getting to know each other. I couldn't have asked for better friends. [URL=http://imgur.com/viXSQ.png][IMG]http://imgur.com/viXSQl.png[/IMG][/URL] [URL=http://imgur.com/jcvxN.png][IMG]http://imgur.com/jcvxNl.jpg[/IMG][/URL] The next day, I bid farewell to my fishy friends, and set out on a long trek. I traveled into my mine, and found plenty of gold and diamond. I rushed home to tell my fish the good news, and... [URL=http://imgur.com/ODTGV.png][IMG]http://imgur.com/ODTGVl.jpg[/IMG][/URL] The fish were gone. I was heartbroken. Even thought we had just gotten to know each other, I felt that we had shared something special, a friendship that would have lasted longer than our lives could contain. Sadly, theirs were cut short. This is a formal appeal to Notch, to please change the item system, so that my fish do not disappear when I leave. I just want them to be happy. In memory of Mr. Fish.
