[QUOTE=Ninjarooster;25921323]Hey Brt5470, I wanted to ask you something about your planet images. How do you make a panoramic photo? Do you use Photoshop or other software?[/QUOTE] I take about 50-60 images scanning left to right up and down covering every angle, then I bring it into a program called PTGUI, it aligns the images into a 2:1 360degree by 180 degree panorama, then I bring it into photoshop. I Increase the vertical resolution to match the horizontal resolution. Then I rotate the image 180degress. Go to filter>Distort>Polar Coordinates, use the default one, hit OK. Done.
