If you place a block, place a fence on it, remove said block, and put a fence where the block was, it makes like a thin pole.
[QUOTE=spah-durr;25865248]What are those thin poles running along the entrence? Theres a pole attached to a fence as well. What is that?[/QUOTE] Stacked fence posts.
[QUOTE=spah-durr;25865248]What are those thin poles running along the entrence? Theres a pole attached to a fence as well. What is that?[/QUOTE] You can stack fences if you do it right. Stack dirt or another block up to one block beneath where you want the top of you fence then put a fence on top. Break the next block down and put a fence there as weel. Continue until you're satisfied. Useful for stuff like those poles. Ninja'd.
