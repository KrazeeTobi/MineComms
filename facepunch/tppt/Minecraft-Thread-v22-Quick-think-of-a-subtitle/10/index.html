[QUOTE=WolfeClaw;23113915]Would a kind person please explain to me how to make custom textures? :3:[/QUOTE] 1. Get WinRAR 2. Go to "C:\Users\<your account>\AppData(hidden folder)\Roaming\.minecraft\bin" 3. Open "minecraft.jar" with WinRAR 4. Create a folder somewhere called "Minecraft Textures" 5. Extract all png/gif files in there, as well as the folders: "armor" "art" "gui" "item" "misc" "mob" "terrain" to the folder you created. 6. Edit them and save. 7. Drag and drop everything from your "Minecraft Textures" folder back into the jar file that is opened with WinRAR (following the same folder structure) and let them replace the others. 8. Play and enjoy :D
