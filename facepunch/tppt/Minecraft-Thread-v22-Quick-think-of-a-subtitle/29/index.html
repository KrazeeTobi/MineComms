Both teams have 5 minutes to set up, in which time they can do any normal Minecraft stuff, but can not go into the enemy's territory. 3. When the game starts, a pig with a saddle in the middle of the map. 4. The pig [i]can[/i] be killed, but has a lot of health, so is not easy to kill. It drops three meat. 5.
