Ok, getting above 32p: 1. Download [url=http://thefwcentral.com/upload/content/other/ijc856991.class]this file[/url], save as ij.class. 2. Open minecraft.jar (located in %appdata%/.minecraft/bin) with 7-zip or WinRAR. 3. Drag and drop the new file in. 4. [b]Delete the META-INF folder.[/b] This is [b][u]very[/u][/b] important. 5. Play! (In the standalone client) If you want to play in the browser again or don't care about such higher quality textures, just delete the bin folder and the normal jars will be redownloaded.
