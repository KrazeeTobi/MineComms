Step 2: Find coal and make glass. Step 3: Use the glass to make the walls of your house. Step 4: Fill up the house with dirt, leaving 1 block for room for the roof/ceiling. Step 5: Put the roof on. Step 6: Make a tube up to the surface. Step 7: Fill the tube with dirt, and mine out the dirt. Step 8: Mine out the dirt. Voila! You have an underwater house.
