How can I get a Redstone Torch to light up when something is on the panel it's wired to? It's on at first so it turns off instead.
Use an extra block&torch set to invert the value. Like this: RBTBT where R is the powder, B is any block, and T is a redstone torch. The last torch will be on when the powder is.
