[quote=dill6869]Still didn't fully explore it yet [/quote] I used to not explore big caves because I would always be afraid that I would get lost and have to spend hours getting out. Here's what you do: First step is to bring a lot of dirt with you. A lot. Next, every time you feel you are getting lost, start jumping and placing dirt under you. If you collide with a block on top of you, destroy it. Keep going up, place temporary torches if you can't tell what block type is above you. Once you get to the surface keep going up. Stack until you have a very noticable pillar in the sky. Place torches all around it (you're making it so that you can see it from very far off). Build a staircase around it to get back down to the ground. What this does is creates a bunch of tall pillars on the surface all around your mine. This helps you with two things, 1. Now you can navigate your mine from above ground, and 2. Every time you get lost you can just add another pillar, and once you get to the surface the other pillars can guide you back home. This can be time consuming but I find it's the best way to navigate enormous caves that take up several mb of save file space.
