[b]The [highlight]MOBS![/highlight] And what they do.[/b] [highlight][b]Enemy Mobs [/b][/highlight] [url=http://www.minecraftwiki.net/wiki/Mobs#Creepers][img]http://ville.hakli.fi/images/OP/creepface.png[/img][/url][b]Creepers![/b] Creepers are the green things with four legs, they simply walk up do you and explode when they are close enough. They drop powder which can be used to make TNT along with sand. I warn you watch out for the SSSSSSsss! [url=http://www.minecraftwiki.net/wiki/Mobs#Skeletons][img]http://ville.hakli.fi/images/OP/skeleface.png[/img][/url][b]Skeletons![/b] Skeletons are the walking bones you see, and they love to shoot arrows. Just strafe them and land a couple hits with your sword. They love to drop their arrows (random amount). These can be used with bows made from string dropped by spiders. [url=http://www.minecraftwiki.net/wiki/Mobs#Spiders][img]http://ville.hakli.fi/images/OP/spiderface.png[/img][/url][b]Spiders![/b] Spiders are the bane of every miner's existance. They jump really high and can usually outrun you. They are wide though so they have trouble getting through doorways. These drop string so you can make bows. Use arrows from skeletons with these. [url=http://www.minecraftwiki.net/wiki/Mobs#Zombies][img]http://ville.hakli.fi/images/OP/zombieface.png[/img][/url][b]Zombies![/b] Zombies are really not much of a threat, but can attack you from above and kill you. They drop feathers for whatever reason which are used to make arrows. [url=http://www.minecraftwiki.net/wiki/Mobs#Slimes][img_thumb]http://www.minecraftwiki.net/images/thumb/e/e8/Slime1.png/180px-Slime1.png[/img_thumb][/url][b]Slimes![/b] Slimes are the smallest mob when in baby form. Baby slimes grow in Peaceful mode, but are friendly. They can grow to be 5x5+ big and are friendly in baby form. Slimes can ONLY be found in certain chunks of your map, so if you're wondering why you never see them down in your mine, you should move your mine to a different chunk and check there. [highlight][b]Friendly Mobs[/b][/highlight] [url=http://www.minecraftwiki.net/wiki/Mobs#Pigs][img]http://ville.hakli.fi/images/OP/Pigface.png[/img][/url][b]Pigs![/b] Pigs are friendly creatures which give you pork when you kill them. Pork can be used to heal, and if you throw it into the furnace it gives you a great health boost. If you find a sattle, you can have a fun time riding them. [url=http://www.minecraftwiki.net/wiki/Mobs#Sheep][img]http://ville.hakli.fi/images/OP/sheepface.png[/img][/url][b]Sheep![/b] Sheep are friendly and will drop wool/cloth, which is mostly useless. It is useful if you need some blocks and get bothered with dirt, wood or anything else. [url=http://www.minecraftwiki.net/wiki/Mobs#Cows][img]http://ville.hakli.fi/images/OP/cowface.png[/img][/url][b]Cows![/b] Cows drop leather when killed, and leather is used to make armor. They also double as musical instruments. [url=http://www.minecraftwiki.net/wiki/Mobs#Chickens][img]http://ville.hakli.fi/images/OP/chikenface.png[/img][/url][b]Chickens![/b] Chickens drop eggs from time to time, eggs have no use as of right now.
