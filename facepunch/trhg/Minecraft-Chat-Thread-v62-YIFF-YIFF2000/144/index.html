If you don't want to just build, then adventure. Explore a virtually endless cave, and when you think you've come to a dead end, dig through it. Keep digging until you hit something or until you've come across a nice supply of iron ore and coal. Envision a structure; a tower or keep of sorts. Your own dream shelter. Not your average hole in the wall; but if you start from a hole, work your way to bigger and better things. Turn that hole into a literal bunker or even a massive ICBM silo. Sure, you can't get ICBMs in MC, but you can build your own silo and furnish it however you please. Maybe you can build something around a natural phenomenon within the game. Right now I'm constructing something within a sort of cavern by the sea. I'm not sure where it'll lead, but it will get somewhere. With time and effort, I'll have made something amazing or destroy it all due to errors and faults, but I'll still have time to create something that will leave myself or others in awe. Your patience and creativity are the only limit to how much you can enjoy Minecraft.
