[QUOTE=Esteam;29060822]zombie fortress[/QUOTE] I wish someone made a decent zombie survival SMP mod. Incoming idea: Everyone spawns on a map with borders, right in the middle. They start building the time they get on. Day is as long as normal, but night is 1.5 times the usual. You are barraged with random mobs for the entire night. The Mobmaster has the ability to spawn mobs at point, but not within 5 or so blocks of the player. They can walk anywhere above the map, fly, or do whatever, but cannot do anything but spawn mobs or possibly something else if they kill enough players. He/she is invisible to everyone, the DM in a way. Every time a player kills a zombie, they get 1g. Every time you kill 20 zombies, you get an extra 10g for a bit of extra spending money. This can be used to buy upgrades such as arrows that do more damage, possibilities of finding apples from leaves, a chance of getting 2x the material from digging it out, enabling you to make a single diamond block you've place previously a healer of sorts, etc. etc. Special weapons could be constructed. Gold tools would not be allowed to be crafted in-game, but you could buy them. For instance, the gold hoe can heal players you hit with it, pickaxe could double the drops of mined materials, axes could do lots of damage to mobs (but cause 1 second cooldown for all attacks!), golden shovels could set mobs on fire on a hit, and a golden sword would cause massive damage, but harm you a little bit. You have three lives. When you die, you lose one. Die three times, you lose all your money and spawn as a "monster". They don't have any different skin, but should somehow have some sort of identifier that they are a monster, like a /nick. You can earn gold for damaging players or killing them, which can be used to buy upgrades. Eventually, as a monster, you can earn the ability to spawn monsters, shoot fireballs, set up more mob spawners, destroy all torches within an x-mile radius, and such. From a standpoint of coding, the monster GUI could simply be normal, but with a different item in each inventory slot. Maybe different kinds of wool. Every time you die, you go to a waiting room and use some /commands to switch out tools you bought, which would replace things in the inventory. When you step in the portal or whatever to be a monster again, it would check how many items you had and if you had repeats. I'm trying to create this idea in a way that it is possible with today's minecraft modding technology. Once again, I state that I have (in my eyes) great ideas, but have horrible execution of them.
