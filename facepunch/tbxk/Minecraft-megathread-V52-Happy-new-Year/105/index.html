[QUOTE=Penis Colada;27276913]Anyone got any tips for underwater building/water removal?[/QUOTE] You can make a 1x1x2 post of dirt, and then put ladders / signs on it to create an air pocket. To drain an area quickly, but waste resources, you can put a glass / stone case around an area that's full of wool or wood. When it's encased, you can set fire to the wood / wool to quickly empty the area that's full of water. I think you can use pumpkins for light, and lightstone too - though that's pricey to make.
