My ideas for things I would like added to Minecraft, but probably won't be. A way to move between save files while in-game. Instead of hell portals, you could build a rocket. It would take you to a space screen with planets. You could found new planets (open save files) or travel to colonized planets (other save files you have). It would all have the minecraft visual style to it, I'm not imagining a mass effect style star-map. Inspired by the recent mod with npc villages and the likes, I think it woud be amazing if as you built buildings of your own in a certain lay-out or with certain requirements, npcs would start to move into your city. You could give these npcs jobs such as mining in defined locations, collecting resources, farming in defined locations, or simply utilizing resources to make buildings or tools. There could be npc factions as well that would expand their kingdom and fight each other. Sort of like Settlers, except first-person. When seeing what others have done with minecraft I feel like those two ideas are quite plausible. I had to put them down, just for fun.
