This is Night Five of our adventures in 2b2t: My friend and I wandered eastward, searching for Putzberg's ruins, and in the process discovered a large tower on an artificial island. We docked here; the [i]Oceanos[/i] drifted away from the shore, my friend accidentally shipwrecked his boat, and night was falling, so we took up shelter here. The entire island was half-quarry, half-tower. Underneath the tower, we found someone's abandoned wolf, still faithfully sitting there, the third wolf I've encountered so far. After a night there, we took our belongings and continued to sail. Unfortunately, we encountered land once more, and we were forced to abandon both of our ships. After trekking overland through some patchy forests, we built new ships and set off as night fell, the [i]Oceanos II[/i] piloted by me. [IMG]http://i.cubeupload.com/esErT2.png[/IMG] We encountered a chokepoint between two islands. The [i]Oceanos II[/i] was lost to a whirlpool; I crafted the [i]Oceanos III[/i] and continued on our way. We found a mine in the side of a mountain, stopped to explore, found some Coal, and then left. Currently, we're holding out in a 2x2 hole-in-the-ground. Is there anyone who's willing to take two trustworthy Facepunchers?
Something that occurs to me; large fixed bases = bad idea. Every time we've built a large fort, it becomes a hub, and then been destroyed/rendered inoperable within a matter of a few weeks. Suggest building many smaller simple outposts, and to keep moving between them. Destruction of a single outpost will mean little. Also chests seem like a bad choice for long term storage, with the high number of x-ray users about. Possibly only use them for temporary storage until your inventories free up?
