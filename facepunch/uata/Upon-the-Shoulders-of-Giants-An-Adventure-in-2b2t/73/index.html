I did, however, find a strange cobblestone-sealed hole in the wall. I broke through, and saw a tunnel down to water. I jumped in. I looked around, and saw that it was a multi-tiered water system underground, and at the end was some lava over signs, with an exit. At first it didn't register in my mind. Then, I turned around and saw a large, completely dark room, and realized: [i]My god, I'm in a fucking mob grinder[/i]. I turned to escape out the end, to be greeted by a skeleton's arrow to the face. I threw down some dirt as cover from the mobs that were already trapped in the grinder, and fended off some zombies until I could get through the grinder. At the end I broke my way through the collection area to safety, letting go some lava behind me to stop any mobs from following. When I got up the ladder, there was a huge room full of chests, and I was horrified to see that the player was in the room with me. I ran to one of the chests and hastily grabbed some arrows for my recently crafted bow, just as the player turned around and saw me. We had a bow duel, and I eventually prevailed. I escaped through his secret exit, and ran as far away from there as I could.
