Ok, new ideas here. First up, meet the Hammer. Comprised of three Sticks and four Wood/Gold/Stone/Iron/Diamond, this a relatively expensive weapon. What the Hammer lacks for in damage it makes up for with a special ability concerning Mobs. If you whack a Mob with the Hammer, they will end up being unable to move, as if they were stunned, leaving them vulnerable for a second (increases slightly with better Hammers), giving you time to either run or sword them. In theory it could also stop a Creeper from going off, so it could end up being a lifesaver. It could also be used to transform a Dirt block into a Mud half-step, making for rather cheap Steps and a reliable source of Mud, which in turn could be used to create Mudballs, a potential item for future recipes... Next on the agenda, the Living Statue. This creature add new spice to the Mob roster, as it acts differently depending on the light level. If the Light is more-or-less at it's highest, it is frozen in place, a stone statue, but when light level drops below a certain point, the statue comes to life and starts attacking you. Now, here's a twist: the Sword doesn't do as much damage to this creature of stone, so instead you must attack it with your Pickaxe, as that's what you use against Stone and stuff. But what it drops depends on it's state when you kill it. If you kill it when it's up and about, it will drop stuff like Sand, Cobblestone, Gravel, Dirt, mundane stuff, but if you destroy it when it's a stationary statue, it will drop more valuable stuff, such as Iron, Gold, Lapis Lazuli, and even Diamond, although that's rare. And another thing: we really should have Pigmen villages soon, with different tribal markings to signify randomly-generated tribes.
