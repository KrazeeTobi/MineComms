[QUOTE=rapperkid04;23912166]I saw someone on the minecraft forums talking about a TNT cannon. How does one make/obtain this?[/QUOTE] To make a tnt cannon, you need to have a bunch of tnt started at once with redstone, and have it set so they fall into water that pushes them towards the tnt projectile. To fire it, activate the propellant tnt, wait a few seconds, and then activate the projectile tnt. The water will stop the propellant tnt from destroying the cannon, and the amount of time you wait to activate the projectile tnt will determine how long the tnt will fly before it explodes.
