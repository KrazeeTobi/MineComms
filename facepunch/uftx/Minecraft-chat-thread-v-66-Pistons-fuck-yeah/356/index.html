CinemaCraft 3 is close to hitting 250k views. [QUOTE=uitham;32826160]You don't notice it when you have your monitors set up like that I think[/QUOTE] Exactly. People forget that with Eyefinity and surround you're actually NOT supposed to look at the side monitors, but only the middle. The side monitors add peripheral information. Also people need to choose the FOV that matches their relative distance to monitor size ratio. If you're close to your monitor, use a large FOV. If you're far from your monitor use a smaller FOV Both of those depend on the size as well. You want the lines from your perspective to line up with the lines inside of the game so that the perspective is sort of like a window or portal into the game. When I had my 30" monitor I would play on Quake Pro as I was only about 2 feet from the screen.
