When a mob spawner gets ready to spawn a mob, it will check the zone to see how many of the monster's type are in the zone (about 5-7?). If it is at the max, it won't spawn. How most traps work is that they keep the player as close to the spawners as possible but lift the mobs out of the mob spawner zone so that it can keep shooting out mobs. How mine works is it uses a mobavator to lift them up the 22 blocks and then drops them, rather than dropping them outright.
