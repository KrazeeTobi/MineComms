this might be old news, but hopefully not. I accidentally stumbled upon these 2 [i]new[/i] blocks when trying to spawn a sandstone slab, though they're not exactly new because all they are are stoneslab blocks with the top texture on the side as well. Here's an image I made displaying them and a few notes regarding it, read below a better explanation [img]http://u.filepak.com/nfFO_blocks.png[/img] Anyway, to get the half blocks you need to be able to spawn blocks. The server I was on which allowed it had /i to do it, /give might work too though. Then you type in "/i stoneslab:4" or "/i 44:4", :5 works too but it's just the same block (not sure about any other numbers) For the doubleslab blocks, you must have world edit (or maybe another similar program, all I know is it works with worldedit). While you can spawn it in your inventory, when you place it it will just turn into a normal doubleslab block. Also, very important, spawn them into your hotbar, because if you don't they'll be stuck in your inventory because when you mouse-over the icon, it crashes your minecraft client. Having it spawn in your hotbar allows for you to scroll onto it and place it.
