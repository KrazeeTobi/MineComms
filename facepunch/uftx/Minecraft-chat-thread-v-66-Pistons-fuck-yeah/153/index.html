[img]http://filesmelt.com/dl/2011-08-18_21.01_.21_.png[/img] Basicly, how it works is it has a giant 22 by 22 hole, and it has platforms going through it, each 3 lower than the one above it, and in a pattern so no mobs drop directly to the bottom level. If a drop will cause a mob damage, it will avoid moving off, so this helps them move along pretty fast. The bottom level is full of water currents going to a 2 by 2 hole in the middle that goes down 25 blocks (It only needs 23, but I went 25 for safety) and they fall on extended pistons, killing them instantly. I have a long clock, and every so often the pistons unextend and drop the items into a current to be collected. (Incase a mob drops in right as it's open, there's a lava blade for him.) Currently, this is my best mobtrap, and I've made a few.
