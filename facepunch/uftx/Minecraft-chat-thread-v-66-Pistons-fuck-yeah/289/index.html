So I generated some land, cut out the parts I wanted and created Eternal Island. Not only does it have 1.8's strongholds, ravines, and abandoned mines, but it also has some unique dungeons spread out around the island. Some are buried deep below, and others have outside entrances in the form of ancient ruins and monuments. I've done my best to give the island some history.
