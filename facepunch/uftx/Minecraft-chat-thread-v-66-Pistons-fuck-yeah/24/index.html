[QUOTE=SGTNAPALM;31211434]Rated tool. Also, shouldn't the FPS be capped at a certain limit? I've read around that it should be (monitor refresh rate)2 + 1.[/QUOTE] There's only 2 FPS caps in Minecraft, one caps it to 40fps, the other to 90fps. Both caps use the extra time for chunk loading/generation/updates, but if you have a fast computer that gets done very quickly anyway. You can force Vsync in your graphics card drivers though, triple buffered is the best. It'll never go over 60fps (or whatever your monitor refresh rate is), but it'll be smooth on any framerate below that as well. I've bumped up the graphical settings via the driver as high as I can go on my GTX 570, and even then it maxes out on 130fps @ 1920x1080.
