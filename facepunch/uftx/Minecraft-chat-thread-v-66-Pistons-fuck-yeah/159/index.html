Also notice that Forge isn't an actual compilation, it's just a mod that works to bring together the other mods; you have to download them separately. This was so that the mod-makers still got to keep their beloved adf.ly links and penny's-worth of revenue. They're all immature as fuck so this was to be expected, really.
