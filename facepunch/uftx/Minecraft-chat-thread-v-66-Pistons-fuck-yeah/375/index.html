[QUOTE=Mattyyyy;33007220]Well, at the moment it's a 50k/hour one, but I should, theoretically, be able to add 2* more of the same onto it, with it still working. [editline]28th October 2011[/editline] I just started testing my mob farm. A stack of each drop in under a minute, but it's still not fast enough.[/QUOTE] If you have two identical traps next to each other, their combined output won't be 2X the output of the trap on its own. The mob limit and mob spawning algorithm won't work for you in that way. If you are able to increase the spawning space by 200% you'll be lucky to increase the item output by 50, not due to a bottleneck in the grinder, but a bottleneck in the way minecraft handles mobs. The key is to kill the mobs ASAP. In my 102k/hour trap a soon as they spawn they are pushed off and die from fall damage, so they die less than a second after they spawn. That's definitely the key - reducing the time from spawn to death.
