[url=http://www.mediafire.com/download.php?cwi8tooxwm31wk3]Download[/url] [media]http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4CqTi0-NNHw[/media] To install: -Ensure you have ModLoader installed properly. -Download rar, and drag the .class files into your minecraft.jar -Put all your .schematic files in "appdata\.minecraft\prints". If it doesn't exist it will be created when the game is run. -Ingame, press "P" and type in your .schematic file name. The settings: -"Bypass adjacent blocks" will place blocks even without a block being adjacent to it. Note that THIS WILL trigger anticheat systems! -"Solve unreachable blocks" will attempt to place "bridges" to blocks that have no adjacent blocks. The bridges are limited to one block. The bridges will ALWAYS be made out of dirt, so if this option is on, ensure you have dirt in your immediate inventory. Planned features: -Pause/resume printing Known issues: -Any blocks that have an item ID that is different from their block ID will not work (redstone, levers, etc.) -Printing will not stop when you leave a world.
