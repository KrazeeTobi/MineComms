[QUOTE=PrusseluskenV2;32169222]I don't really understand why the Far Lands even exist. Why is there a limited amount of X and Z blocks you can travel, anyway?[/QUOTE] It's due to how floating point values are handled by computers, as the value increases the accuracy decreases. So at really high values, the error level starts becoming large enough that calculations don't add up correctly (since the number is smaller or larger than it should be). Notch has ideas on how to fix it (Easy enough, make distances relative to the chunk instead of the origin, etc.), but since it's so far away, and players like exploring it, he said he probably won't fix it until it becomes an actual problem for normal players.
No one can really legitimately get to the farlands without extreme dedication, I don't even know how the first person got there.
