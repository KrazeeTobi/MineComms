[QUOTE=RedStar;33939195]Bit late replying, but how do I do this?[/QUOTE] You can edit the kingdommod.prop file and change this: ReficuleFireSpawning{0\=yes,1\=no}=1 to this: ReficuleFireSpawning{0\=yes,1\=no}=0 and this: ReficuleNetherrackSpawning{0\=yes,1\=no}=1 to this: ReficuleNetherrackSpawning{0\=yes,1\=no}=0 Now, I am not sure if this works properly since I haven't met any reficules when I changed that as I already had my kingdom set, so you'd have to check that yourself, I just assumed that this should work.
