Remember the mod evil minecraft? Those modders are going to add up to 42,000,000 new weapons to the game. Yes, you heard it right. And all this is possible because this little tweet by notch: [quote]Yeeesss!! Arbitrary NBT Data per Item Instance is done! Works in both single player and multi player. :D :D[/quote] It is hard to explain what this means, but basically it allows many stuff. Have a quote directly from the website for more info (I recommend to read it all) [quote]Notch recently tweeted about finishing arbitrary NBT data per item instance into the game. This opens quite large doors for us, and we're taking full advantage of them. We're going to be adding a new weapon system that allows us to have tens of millions of unique weapons. Some details are outlined below. [b]How it Works[/b] It's quite simple. Using the new data system, we're redoing how weapons themselves work. Each weapon will be comprised of up to five 'parts': Grade Prefix Element Material Weapon type [b]Grade[/b] A weapon's grade is quite simple. It's a number ranging from -10 to +10. There haven't been specifics decided yet, but the grade of a weapon will likely affect both damage and durability of a weapon. It will not have any physical change on a weapon. [b]Prefix[/b] The prefix of a weapon is probably the coolest part of this all. There will be around 250 prefixes, randomly chosen upon a weapon's 'generation'. Just note that this part of a weapon is optional and may sometimes not even be there. This changes the weapon's appearance, generally. A few examples: Rusty - Slightly decreases weapon damage but also gives it a small amount of poison damage. Drilling - Acts like a pickaxe as well as a weapon. Glowing - Gives you the 'torch in hand' effect while wielding the weapon. Element This is the 'magic' or 'enchantment' part of a weapon. We're going to be adding around 50 different elements for a weapon to have. Much like the prefix, this is also optional and sometimes won't be there. The change this has on a weapon is based on the element. Some will make it emit particles of some sort, and some will have animated textures. A few examples: Lava - High-level flame element. Sets mobs on fire as well as having highly increased damage. Has an animated texture. Toxic - Poisonous element. On-hit has a high chance of putting the 'poison' effect on an enemy. The weapon emits a 'misty' sort of particle effect around it. [b]Material[/b] This is much like what the current weapon system simply is. The weaker the material, the lower the durability and damage. The stronger, the higher durability and damage. The material won't be shown in an item's name, rather in appearance only. We'll be adding some more materials to round the number up to 20. [b]Weapon Type[/b] In addition to swords, we'll be adding new weapon types as well as allowing tools to act better as weapons with the right prefixes/elements. There will end up being around 8 'tools'(including things like swords and such) in the end. Just note that a lot of this is subject to change, but it will be around 42,000,000 unique items. Try it out! We've opened a generator located on our [url=http://evil-minecraft.net/weapongen]Weapon Name Generation[/url] page and it has a tweet button! Try to reach the goal of 42,000,000 unique combinations!
