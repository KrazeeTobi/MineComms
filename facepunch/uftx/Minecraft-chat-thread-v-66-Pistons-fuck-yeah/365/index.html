Hey guys. I wrote a Minecraft explanation fanfic (overused topic, I know), so I'd thought I'd post here. Constructive criticism, please. For years, the NeePeces had lived in the land of grassy hills and snow-topped mountains in peace and unison. Each one had their own job and duty to fulfill; whether it be tending to the precious crops, or perhaps even feeding the livestock. Their robes symbolized their title, from green to red, black to blue. Their land was fertile and fresh. An abundance of animals roamed the land, from dawn to dusk. Hills spouted up, mountains sat firmly in the northern hemisphere. From green plains to the bluest of oceans, their world was in a perfect cycle that we call &#8220;Nature&#8221;. However, their land was so gentle only because of its position in the rest of the world. It was merely an undiscovered mass of land that was placed somewhere between North and South America. The other parts of Earth were too busy with their senseless wars and massacres that they did not bat an eye at the sight of a seemingly unpopulated country. The occasional message in a bottle and stray boat did come along once in a while, though. Not all the boats were unpopulated, though. A man in a blue-green, slightly worn out, shirt and faded blue jeans had arrived, from where he came from was uncertain, legends foretold that he was simply just out looking for adventure. Humans are nasty pieces of work. Their immune systems have evolved in such a way that illnesses that can slaughter an entire barn full of cattle will only cause a man to sneeze. Once this man had arrived onto the island, he immediately started to chop down the trees and tear apart the overgrown grass. The NeePeces watched in horror, but because of their peaceful nature, did not attempt to stop this act of greed. His flesh was tainted, though. He was a carrier of a virus that we now call &#8220;the common cold&#8221;. This horrible bug almost wiped out all of the villages, only the farthest ones out are still remaining, albeit smaller then they were before. The much closer villages responded much more differently to the disease. The preacher's skin had melted away, the cook's had mutated into an unholy shade of green. The hunters refused to fight their &#8220;fallen&#8221; comrades, and as such, fell to the virus, too. They grew about another foot, their flesh mutating into a dark shade of purple. They had devolved into spiders, quick and swift. Their livestock suffered the worst fate of all. The pigs began to stand on their hind legs, their eyes shriveled up. The once pig's skin had morphed into the impurest type of green you can get. They hide in the shadows, awaiting the mysterious Man. Once they find their target and calculate a strategic plan, they strike: The demons explode after latching onto His back like a parasite, a suicide technique. Sometimes it works, other times it fails. Today, the Man still survives. He shall turn the land into His own, dominating all the beasts and animals He may find. The NePeces still exist, too, but are on the verge of extinction. But who am I, you may ask? I am the force that drives you insane. I am the force that takes back our land. I am the force that watches you wisely. I am the force that lurks in the night. I am the force that does not affiliate with Villages, Man, or Monster. I am the force that can cause quite a stir. I am the force that you underestimate I am Enderman, warrior of Ender.
