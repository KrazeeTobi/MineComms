Alright, so I thought I'd share with you guys the minigame for SMP that I've been making for the past couple hours. The game is based around either knocking your opponents off the platforms or killing them by hitting them with your sword. The second way gives you more points (diamonds) as you get all the diamonds from the person you killed, assuming they had any. The other way just gets you one point per round. When people join the server, they spawn in the lounge, which is this area: [t]http://i.cubeupload.com/v4xHlm.png[/t] If there are still open doors, you go inside one, flick the switch and then wait on top of the gold block. [t]http://i.cubeupload.com/fuMMa8.png[/t] After all 4 teams have flicked their switches, the dispenser will give you a sword and then the floor will open, leading into the arena where all 4 teams will fight.
