[QUOTE=New Cidem;31389877]Very well then. How are the hip younguns making their mines these days? I can't much explain the way I do it, but what I can say is that it isn't very effective and I'd like to know a better way to do it.[/QUOTE] Dig a staircase down to bedrock, then go up 11 levels. This will mean you will be above any lava pools so any lava you encounter will be from the rare flows you encounter at any level. You are below level 16 so you will encounter every ore including diamond. Dig a branch like design like so - [img]http://dl.dropbox.com/u/5062494/junk/branch_mine.png[/img] ^ This is a birds eye view Every tunnel should be 2 tall and 1 wide, and the branches should be spaced 3 blocks apart. This is because almost all ore veins are 2 blocks wide, so you'll mine the smallest amount of stone for the most mineral. Each branch can be as long as you like but I recommend about 20 blocks long. Just make the main passage as long as you need. You can expand it forever really. Or if it gets too long you can just turn one of the branches close to the entrance into a second main passage.
