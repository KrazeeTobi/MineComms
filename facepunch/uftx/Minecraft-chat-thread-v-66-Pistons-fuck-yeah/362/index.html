[QUOTE=hoodoo456;32890140]How do you make a slime farm?[/QUOTE] Basically you'll want to break all slimes down into the smallest version (I'm using a 1 high layer of signs with 3 layers of water on top. Effectively drowns the big slimes but spares the small ones) You then want to send the small slimes to a 1x1 shaft going to the surface, with ladder all the way. At the surface you create the final part to kill the little slimes. You could kill them underground, but if you let them climb you won't have to go through the hassle of transporting the slimeballs yourself.
