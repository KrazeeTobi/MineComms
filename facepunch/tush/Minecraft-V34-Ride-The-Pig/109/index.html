a little bit late here, but i forced 8x antialiasing onto minecraft [img]http://dl.dropbox.com/u/7539801/aax8.png[/img] it worked relatively well as you can see, despite a few small bugs i also learned it's [b]MOSTLY[/b] not forced antialiasing causing the pink lines on water, it's forced anistropic filters. anistropic filtering decreases the quality of textures when viewed from a distance or from the sides, to prevent noise, or the different parts of the texture "fighting" to be drawn. due to the fact it blurs the texture, it grabs some of the pink from the area beside the water texture on terrain.png, causing the pink to show up.
