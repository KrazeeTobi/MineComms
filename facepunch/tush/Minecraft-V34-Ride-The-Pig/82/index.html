[QUOTE=LewisZeCool;24037833] Cant you have like a wooden one or stone or something, I rarely find enough iron to waste on buckets[/QUOTE] You are not spelunking enough. Step 1: Mass on torches (about 30-50) and a few rock pickaxes. A couple of swords too. Step 2: Find cave entrance on the surface. Step 3: Check cave size from the entrance, if it leads further underground, go to step 4, if not return to step 2. Step 4: Start exploring the cave while placing torches to light your path and mark the way out and killing monsters. Step 5: Find plenty iron ore and mine it. If you don't, return to step 2. Step 6: Gather iron, torches and all the resources you found and leave the cave system.
