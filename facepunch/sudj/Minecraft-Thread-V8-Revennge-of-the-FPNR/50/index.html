I was thinking about buying this game because it looks fun. But, what exactly is it? What is it about? What do you do? I tried watching video but it doesn't really explain much.
Indev is pretty simple. You start off in a house and have until nighttime to build up your house and get materials by hitting trees with your bare fist for wood, mashing the ground for dirt, playing bad-cop with the sand, etc. When night falls, there'll be monsters (unless you set the difficulty to peaceful in which there are no monsters), so it's recommended you go back to your base. You can then make a crafting table and extend your life in Minecraft even more with tools to help you dig and work faster, so you can build up your base and survive. Then when day arrives, you fend off the monsters (or set difficulty to peaceful :v:) and go get more materials. It's a game of build and destroy, basically. The level generator is amazing, and once you see infdev you'll never regret buying the game. In case you didn't notice, I really like Minecraft.
