[QUOTE=Mislumz;20856093]I wish there was a way to build downwards, because playing on Floating with Deep is impossible without having the ability to do that.[/QUOTE] it's TOUGH, but what I do with deep floating is I'll turn it on peaceful and try to find the bottom-most point of my 'island' by digging down a 2x1 tunnel so if I break ONE brick with nothing beneath it, I'll still be standing on another (since I'm standing on the crack, either side will save me). most of the islands have a bottom point that is relatively close to the next one down, so once you find the bottom point and drop, you SHOULD survive the fall... but save your game before hand just in case. to lessen the fall by a little, have some dirt ready when you're falling, look straight down, and hold down left click so when you get close to the ground, you'll place one or two bricks, shortening the overall fall and maybe saving some health. being on peaceful, you regenerate your health and can keep plowing downward. also, keep a workbench and ALL the wood you could gather from the top island on you. when you break your stone pickaxes, drop the workbench, make new tools, then break the workbench to put it back in your inventory. note, breaking chests will not keep their contents inside like some magic pocket-box, but they'll drop all of their contents on the floor... so... don't do THAT if you ever feel the need.
