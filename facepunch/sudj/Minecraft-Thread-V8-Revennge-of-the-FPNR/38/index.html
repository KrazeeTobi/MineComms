Also: Did anyone else notice that Minecraft installs a folder where it keeps all the saves and sounds in the AppData folder? There's a folder called Random. There are sounds like the bow shooting, walking, etc. But i've found a few things: There is a folder called "sound" and "newsound". "newsound" contains things like the sound of lava, water rushing, etc. None of these sounds are used in Indev. There is a sound called "breath". It is a creepy sound of someone taking a very slow breath. I'm pretty sure this is the sound that Creepers make when they've spotted you. There are two sounds called "fizz" and "fuse". I think "fizz" is the Survival sound that Creepers make when they've lit their fuse. "fuse", on the other hand, is the sound that Creepers make in Indev. It is a much more realistic sound, similar to a match being struck, and a fuse lighting. Now, the most mysterious of all: there is a folder called "step". It lists sounds for stepping on things. I went through all the regulars, sand, wood, stone, dirt, etc, but then I saw a few that caught my eye: "snow". Personally, I think Notch is a bit further in development than he'd like us to believe.
