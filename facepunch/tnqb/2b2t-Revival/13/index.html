[QUOTE=ThermalArc;35428149]is it only me or the nether portals are fucked, I can enter any portals but I always exit at the one near the spawn. this is BS edit: anybody need help with their settlement, just bring a bit of food at spawn and escort me outta this shit hole and I'll be your slave[/QUOTE] Portals aren't very complicated, but when you do not know where they all are, it makes them seem broken when they always bring you back to a single portal. Basically, the portals near spawn in the Overworld are the only "nearby" portals to any portals you might find close-by in the Nether. Thus all portals in the Nether will map to the single portal in the Overworld at spawn, until you've traveled a couple hundred blocks through the Nether, which will shift the search distance for locating a corresponding portal in the Overworld out of range of the Overworld spawn portal such that it will either find a new Overworld portal or create one for you to exit. Read up on their usage and linkage if you want to be better informed.
