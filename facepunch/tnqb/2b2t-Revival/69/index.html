[QUOTE=endorphinsam;35980636]Could you explain how to? Because I'm sort of curious now.[/QUOTE] Look for "patch walls" aka. gigantic walls of netherack that go on for long distances and seem completely out of place. That is the separation between different content patches of terrain generation, which seems to have been changed for every patch. Thus by counting those patch walls as you move outwards from spawn, you can roughly determine what patch of terrain you might be in. Nether fortresses will only be found in 1.0 and above patches (about 6-8 patch walls). As for determining if a particular piece of terrain in your current patch has not been explored before, look for the tell-tale signs of player exploration in the nether: 1-block-wide bridges of death. If you see any of those or torches etc. you know someone else has already been there and you must continue looking.
