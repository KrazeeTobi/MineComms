2b2t success story: So I log on to 2b2t today with the intent of visiting 2k2k. After getting out of spawn relatively easy I find a nether portal and start to make my into the negatives. Shortly thereafter I make it to the gutted mass of stone that barely resembles a mountain completely devoid of anything useful. I'm starting to get hungry so I kill a few zombies and pigs for their meat and I decide to go farther in the nether. For a while I travel with a good bit of food searching for another portal to the overworld. It seems like I'm going on forever though these carved paths of others before me. Not too long after that I realize I'm running way low on food. I'm starting to feel like I'm not going to make it out alive when I find a portal but my bad luck reveals it's been broken. I travel on for a little bit more and eat my last piece of zombie meat and I'm really worried I'm not going to make it out alive. But in the distance I see the edge of obsidian over a lava lake so I move to the side to see if it's still working. Yes it is. Now all I have to do is get to it. There's no straight footpath there so I push my luck by going though tunnels mined by others. The one I choose doesn't lead me the right way and I'm now lost in a nether cave without a sense of direction. I start to panic. My hunger bar is halfway gone with no food left. My stone pickaxe is red and I don't know where I came from. I desperately search every direction in this cave looking for a way out but all I'm finding are dead ends. It should have been right there. I saw my exit. How could I have fucked this up. I'm going to die and have to walk out here all over again. But the whole time I was also thinking I was going to make it out alive. Sure enough I somehow find a path out of the cave but at first the portal is nowhere in sight. I move out into the open and spot it again, and this time I can walk to it. I hop in the portal and arrive tens of thousands of blocks away from spawn. I kill a pig and save myself from hunger and I start to mine. I find some iron ore so I make a sword and keep moving. After a few thousand more blocks I stumble upon this strange sight. [thumb]https://dl.dropbox.com/u/25698984/2b2t1.jpg[/thumb] Moving around that I stumble upon this really beaten down house [thumb]https://dl.dropbox.com/u/25698984/2b2t2.jpg[/thumb] Now I'm excited to see this abandoned base at such a far out location but I'm sure it's got nothing of value left inside. I take a look around and find it's mostly empty with broken glass but there are two double chests. Inside I find some normal things like wood coal and redstone but nothing that is really going to make my life easier. I start to cook more pork when I turn around to make sure no mob was at my back. This is when I notice in the corner a pile of items that I hadn't seen there before. Out loud I say "no fucking way" as I see the hint of cyan diamond laying there on the ground mixed in with the gray of iron and red of tnt. I couldn't believe this had happened. I found a diamond sword, 50 iron bars, 13 diamonds, a full set of iron armor. This will never happen to me again. I didn't know what to do with my newly found riches so I just exited the game and wrote this here. [img]https://dl.dropbox.com/u/25698984/2b2t3.jpg[/img]
