My server-hosting friend decided to start a survival event last year, where everyone started out fresh on a fresh map and it was basically FFA, first player to the End wins. There were about 10 people at most, I think, if not less, and because we were all friends or friends of friends, there wasn't much PVPing. Plenty of mischief, like thieving from bases and the occasional kill, but nothing malicious. The core group of people - me, the host, my best friend, and someone else I can't remember - had pretty much established bases and mines the first day, and eventually we were secure with things like guarded nether portals (I built a lava shield around mine, which basically fucked everyone else over) and diamond armor and such. Two or three days after the server starts, at a point where most of us are at iron or diamond tier, a "friend of a friend of a friend" gets on. The guy was someone I was acquainted with in real life but couldn't stand to talk to, because he was just so... [I]stupid.[/I] It was frustrating at times. Anyway, he gets on and starts building a little mud hut and pestering us for how-to information (we told him to use the wiki) and starts snooping around people's bases. He also had a habit of broadcasting whenever he did something, i.e. "look guyz i found dimonds", "how do i make the encanting table cus i gots lot of xp". I was preparing for the journey to the End in secret at this point, and I needed XP so I went and ambushed the guy. I had diamond gear by then and he had no idea what was going on, so it was kind of a dick move on my part, but the game was FFA as we had all agreed on. The guy goes nuts and starts accusing me of hacking, complaining directly to the host and asking him to ban me, all sorts of shit, but the host could pull up the logs and show that there was no evidence that I had cheated. He wouldn't shut up though, and complained well into the end of the game and into the next world event, which was using Tekkit. The endgame for [I]that[/I] was him nuking my base with stuff that he [I]had[/I] cheated in. :v: tl;dr: I ambushed some guy in a FFA survival game and he calls hacks, then becomes a massive hypocrite.
