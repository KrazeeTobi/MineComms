[QUOTE=Fury McFlurry;41735303]How do I craft iron tools with tinkers constructs?[/QUOTE] Smeltery. You need lots of clay, gravel, sand, and your furnace's choice of fuel for grout to cook to seared bricks, then lots of lava for the lava tank. You need enough aluminum and copper for aluminum brass, but three aluminum ores (use gravel ores if you're too scared to go underground yet) and one copper makes enough aluminum brass for two bars of it. Make a stone piece of whatever you need, like the pickaxe head and place it in the casting table, pour aluminum brass and boom, you have a pickaxe head cast. By this point, I hope you used that last aluminum brass on an ingot to make an ingot cast, that'll be your quick removal of leftover metal.
