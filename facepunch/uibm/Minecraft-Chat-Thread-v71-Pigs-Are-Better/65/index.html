Anybody remembers my monologue about Factions servers from page 40? Let me just throw in this one gem and I shall leave this topic forever. (Just so you don't have to ask about how do I know this, this all was told by a friend of mine who still played on the server.) [b][u]---------------[/u][/b] The Admin organized another faction war - Blue versus Orange+Green. You might think "it will be a complete and utter steamroll". And it was. But if you think an alliance of two factions is an unstoppable force, you're wrong. The battle was scheduled to start [i]in a day from the announcement.[/i] The Blues were practically ready, and the Alliance had to hurry up. They managed to get diamond tools and enchantments despite the handicap and that Ores-From-Stone-Only plugin. When the time finally came for the battle, [b]the Blues had 12 players online, meanwhile the Alliance had 7 players,[/b] 4 from one faction and 3 from the another one. Not that it was already a sure steamroll to start by Blue Faction, something was off about the battle - [b]the server started to lag unusually strong [u]just as the battle started.[/u][/b] People were dropping slots every second, [b]but if one was to keep looking at the server dynmap, they'd spot that only the people who were in Green and Orange faction were crashing[/b]. The Blues were just fine. [b]Despite that, the Admin did not stop the war.[/b] The friend of mine dropped and logged back on only to find himself getting slaughtered by two Blues standing just at the spot where he crashed. Needless to say, Flawless Victory for Blues - no members lost, all loot gathered, basically a Curb Stomp Battle. But the real kicker comes now. After the battle, one of the Blues accidentally complimented Hamster, the Blue Faction leader, in the server's thread on some forum with a very specific sentence. The sentence was: [b]"haha gj hamster on those lag machines ;)"[/b] - it was the proof that Blues were using something more than just external scripts - they even lagged the server just to win. [b]This one sentence led to the Green and Orange Leaders resigning from their places and calling the Admin an unfair pile of shit, the Forum Administrator locking down the thread and the Admin's account getting deleted AND the server getting closed by himself in rage.[/b] TL;DR is in bold.
