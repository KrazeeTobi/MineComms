[QUOTE=Furioso;41135868]For those that are handy with redstone, is it possible to make a current travel down blocks vertically? That is to say, without having to make tiers/stairs that the redstone wire can travel down.[/QUOTE] You don't have to make a staircase, but obviously you'll have some type of tiering. You can use sticky pistons pushing blocks of redstone down over redstone dust, which can stack with the dust activating the next piston and so on. If you're short on pistons, redstone torches (on the side of a block) will affect the dust one block below them, and you can alternate from side to side and bring a signal down that way. Has double the footprint, but less material cost.
