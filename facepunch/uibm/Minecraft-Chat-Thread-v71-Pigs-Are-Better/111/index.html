I don't like mods because they feel too different than regular Minecraft. Was playing FTB with a friend of mine once, hit a key (k, I think) and found a garish, obtrusive and seemingly pointless green box that said to "bind keys" by hitting a button and dragging them on the screen. I dunno, it was weird. Neither of us know what it's for, either, because we don't mod.
That would be part of modular power suits, which in general feels too different from regular Minecraft since everything in it looks all future-y (it's still pretty neat looking though). It's also pretty OP in my opinion since once you get enough resources for it, you can be nearly invincible, fly super fast, teleport, instantly kill most enemies with a hitscan weapon, have a pickaxe/axe/shovel that never breaks, and more. Sure, it takes power to run, but there's enough power generation options for it that it rarely becomes relevant. Of course, I realize I'm complaining mostly about the default recipe set, which is what most servers running FTB use. I honestly did not expect me to go into a mini rant about that.
