[QUOTE=redsoxrock;40803788]What good are fences when animals can just walk right out?[/QUOTE] Because they can't. Try it yourself, jump over a fence.
While animals really shouldn't be able to escape fences, if you pack too many of them in one space, I think when the chunk unloads the game places the animal inside the fence and they wander out. I built an automatic chicken farm with Thaumcraft Golems once and I had to fence the area around the chicken coop because chickens kept glitching through the fence. I started killing the grown chickens that got out instead of the ones inside.
I get glitches where it LOOKS like the animals have gotten outside the fence, but then as soon as they move, they snap back inside the fence boundaries.
I think anyone who's built a pen out of solid, two high blocks has had animals mysteriously die from being shoved against the wall and suffocating.
