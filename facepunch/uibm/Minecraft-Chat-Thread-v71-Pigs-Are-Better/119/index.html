I'm using the Magic Launcher, 1.6.4, the latest Forge version, and the latest Erebus version I've tried it on its own and with other mods, but for some reason, having the Erebus activated will make my Minecraft close after the Mojang screen.
There's a borderless fullscreen mod here: [url=https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/u/40716251/Mods/WindowedMode/2013.09.22/2013.09.22.01-1.6.4%20-%20WindowedMode.zip]1.6.4 DEFAULT[/url] | [url=https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/u/40716251/Mods/WindowedMode/2013.09.22/2013.09.22.01-MF1.6.4%20-%20WindowedMode.zip]1.6.4 FORGE[/url] (Credit to Shoukaseiyko: [url]http://www.minecraftforum.net/topic/1816760-164-fullscreen-windowed-forge-compatible/[/url]) [quote] Simply extract inside your minecraft.jar or new launcher equivalent (Delete META-INF too) and press Ctrl+F11 to turn on windowed fullscreen mode. If you don't want the menu opening when you alt-tab, press Ctrl+Alt+F11.[/quote] If you want to add it to FTB, the minecraft jar is located in FTBFOLDER\versions\1.6.4\1.6.4.jar Delete meta-inf too. HOWEVER, when you try to launch FTB, it won't launch cause FML detects the .jar and says it's corrupt (funfact, it isn't). Add this '-Dfml.ignoreInvalidMinecraftCertificates=true' to your launch parameters (options > advanced options) and it will launch fine.
