Factions servers are literally the biggest bullshit I've ever experienced in Minecraft. Let's start my riveting tale of tears and unfairness. On the server, there were three factions - Orange, Blue and Green. I was in the last one, and we did fairly good. But two days after the server's opening (yes, that short) everything began to fall apart. We were being constantly robbed by someone. We decided to build a storage to place unwanted stuff there. They even stole that unwanted pile of items. Our spawn got lava'd, no chances of getting out of there. We were also being constantly accused of "sending thieves, griefers and spies" into other factions (complete bullshit, we didn't ever send anyone). At last we managed to get the admin to reroll our map after a few days. He also rerolled everything that belonged to members - so during the sixth day we were basically at Day 0. The admin also [i]declared a war of Greens and Blues.[/i] We were struggling to get an advantage - no result. Ores were disabled because of that retarded plugin that makes ores drop from stone at random. So we were basically screwed over - a rich map, but we couldn't get any of that. Also, there were no diamonds - you could only get them from the ingame Market (God only knows why this retarded thing of a system was introduced). The only way you could get money was from farming and selling items. Our world didn't have a single piece of grass anywhere near the spawn. Also, no villages. Aaand it was finally the last day - war time. Our faction's equipment consisted of iron armor, swords and all that. Guess what the Blues had? [i]Full diamond armor and equipment, complete with enchantments. Also, Golden Apples and Ender Pearls.[/i] Needless to say, we got completely steamrolled. When I complained to the admin that the fight was completely unfair, he just ignored me and said that I didn't do shit during the fight. [i]Except I was the first one on the battlefield, the rest was just [u]too scared[/u] to move out of the spawn.[/i] Now the Blues are admitting to using external scripts ("calculations"), making AFK Machines (God knows for what, but it's basically bypassing the Anti-Idle plugin) and many other unfair shit. Best part? [b]Even my ex-teammates are turning against me, just because they got convinced by the other factions that I am the cause of all evil.[/b] Bonus points of douchebagness for the admin - the leader of the Blue Faction is his IRL friend. It explains why he did not give a shit about them playing dirty. [b]TL;DR:[/b] Factions servers consist of elitists, cheaters, scumbags and ignorant admins. Basically the worst kind of experience you can have in Minecraft. [sp]Sorry if it's a wrong place to post it, but I just wanted to share the story and making another thread seems pointless imho.[/sp]
