I know there are often posts asking for mods that have a "minecrafty" feel, but I have a twist on that: Are there any mods (for Forge) that replicate the feel of the [i]old[/i] Minecraft, back in Beta? Minecart boosters, terrain gen, auto-fire bows, etc. I know there are mods out there, but all the ones I've ever encountered that did any of the aforementioned things were always incompatible with Forge or out of date.
I think there's a big problem in that nobody can agree what a "minecrafty" feel is. Like for some people, TNT was okay, but adding guns and other gunpowder-themed weapons would ruin the feel of minecraft. Some people are totally fine with minecarts and such, but other vehicles would completely ruin the game. Some people like things like enchanting, potions, and boss battles, so things like Industrialcraft ruin the game for them. Some people like building complex redstone machines, but mods adding electricity are the dumbest shit. Some people think HORSES are going to be the one thing that RUINS the feel of minecraft ONCE AND FOR ALL. So a mod that "feels minecrafty" is hugely subjective.
