I'm mediocre at pixel art, but I've been really wanting to make my own texture pack. I'm considering starting a texture pack that doesn't really have a particular theme and just more so my art style. If it had a theme it'd probably be "Cartoon Gruesome" because I like to make my mobs look colorful and detailed but not realistically detailed. I also like to try and make them look bloody and, well, gruesome. (just the enemy mobs of course) [t]http://i.imgur.com/K654mbI.png[/t] This is the first thing I made. I wanted to give the skeleton mob an actual skull for a head. That was pretty much my main goal for this skin. I'm really happy with it but it looked plain, so I added some blood and cracks. [t]http://i.imgur.com/pnW07nk.png[/t] The Creeper is the second skin I did. I wanted to make it look like some sort of insane creature that had a clever reason for exploding. It's oozing a sort of acidic goo from its mouth. Not only does it have TNT sticks tied to it, but you can see that the interior of its head also has TNT in it (not my original idea obviously.) I also added some skin patterns to it in order to prevent it from looking boring. [t]http://i.imgur.com/AGA8jiq.jpg[/t] The Ghast is always crying out and making horrifying noises, so I figured I'd try and make it look like an extremely depressed and tortured creature. Lots of gashes, cuts, and an arrow stuck in the side of him. Also sorta made it look like its tentacles were damaged by changing what they look like at the bottom. I also gave it a slight pink tone so that it looks more like something you'd see flying around in the midst of the underworld. This still has a lot scheduled to change, I was waving my imagination around when I made this. [t]http://i.imgur.com/EZNFXoD.png[/t] This is an apple. Should I continue? I have a lot of work ahead of me and it's pretty overwhelming. [editline]25th April 2013[/editline] I'd also like to mention that the Cowtoon Texture Pack was a big boost of motivation. I love how that texture pack looks and it really motivated me to get off my ass and start doing this.
