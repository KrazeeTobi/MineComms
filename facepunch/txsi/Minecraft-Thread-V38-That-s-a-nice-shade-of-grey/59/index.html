[QUOTE=ZombieWaffle;24946807]How do I even build anything? I don't have an axe or anything[/QUOTE] Chop down tree with BARE HANDS. X is trunk, * is empty space in crafting section. Make trunk pieces into planks. X* ** X is wood planks, * is empty space in crafting section. Craft four plank pieces into workbench, place workbench on floor. XX XX Craft two plank pieces into sticks. X* X* Open workbench, arrange stuff like so (P = Plank, S = Stick) PP* *S* *S* Instant axe! And just do PPP *S* *S* For a pickaxe.
