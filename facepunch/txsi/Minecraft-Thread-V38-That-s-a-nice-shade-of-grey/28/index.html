I dunno what it is about this game, but it gets to me. I build my guy a nice cosy house with a little paddock for crops, he has a pool and a garden and trees and animals. He has his own little life. I make him live it out every day. Working during the day, adding to his house, tending the crops. Dinner at night and his own little bed. The only thing he hasn't got is somebody else. This game is so lonely, so sad on Single player. At night when I sit in the little bed, I think "This is all his life will be. Sure, he has his house and his pool and crops and animals and all of those nice things. But he doesn't have anybody else." and that makes me pretty sad. It makes me long for companionship for my little guy. Then that music plays. That beautiful, melancholy music. I love this game.
