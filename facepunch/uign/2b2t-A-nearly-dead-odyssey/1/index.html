[IMG]http://imgur.com/HjyiOID.png[/IMG] 2b2t is the longest running and largest minecraft world ever made. There are no rules, no staff, and infinite possibility, in a completely vanilla setting. The world (at the time of writing this) is 787GB large and 42 months old and nearly 60,000 players have wandered the world at least once. The server will continue to grow and age as long as the community pays $90 monthy to maintain it. If you're someone who loves to explore, discover and create this is the server for you, there are a multitude of bases, forms of terrain to explore, and (thanks to a few dozen exploits, and even one back-door over the years) plenty of OP gear, loot, and resources to steal, provided you're lucky and persistent enough to go out there and find it. At peak times the server has around thirty players on, some are friendly, most are deadly. As you'd expect, a server this old has a lot of history. A lot of it is cataloged [URL="http://facepunch.com/showthread.php?t=1083343"]here[/URL], [URL="http://www.facepunch.com/showthread.php?t=1105156"]here[/URL], [URL="http://facepunch.com/showthread.php?t=1160481"]here[/URL], [URL="http://facepunch.com/showthread.php?t=1174308"]here[/URL], and most recently, [URL="http://facepunch.com/showthread.php?t=1250221&page=33"]here[/URL]. A few things to expect; racism, nazism, homophobia, sexism, and a ton of other bad isms. This is a server without rules, after all. If you're having trouble, getting out of spawn, then you are outta luck. I won't be making any sort of tutorials until I have time. There's a handy-dandy [URL="http://the2b2t.blogspot.com/"]blog[/URL] for anyone interested, made by the sexy James_Rustles The IP is 2b2t.org. Good luck, try not to die too much.
