For me, Minecraft has the same gameplay appeal that the Sims does. In both games, Sims and Minecraft, you start off with nothing, but accumulate a fortune. Now once you have a mansion in the Sims, or a Fortress in Minecraft, some players might get bored. However, I like to expand my goals. Built my first house in minecraft out of wood. Now I need to make another house, maybe a bigger one this time. Great, that's done, but now I need to need to lay some cobble stone and connect them. To sum it up; the longer you spend playing the game; the more ambitious your personal projects become. However, I suppose you are looking for some incentive to do that.
