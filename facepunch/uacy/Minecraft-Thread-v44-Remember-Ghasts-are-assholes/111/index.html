I have an idea for a craftable weapon, looking for some feedback. People keep suggesting that notch adds guns, but I think that's a fucking stupid idea. All of the player's tools are rudimentary, not produced by some manufacturing plant. Guns would break the charm that the simple home-made tools add to the game, it's always good to see a game break away from the typical "brown and bloom with guns" formula. That aside, I think there's a common ground here. I propose a reed gun, a one shot weapon made out of a reed (for the barrel), gunpowder, and some sort of ammunition (flint, sticks, iron, etc). It would be differentiated from the bow and arrow by a longer range of fire. The kind of ammunition you craft it with would affect its damage, ranging from wooden sword level to near instant kill. The stronger variations would not be stack-able, so you couldn't craft a bunch of diamond shooting reed guns and kill everything effortlessly. Crafting recipe would be as follows: R = Reed A = Ammunition G = Gunpowder [ ][A][ ] [ ][R][ ] [ ][G][ ] What do you guys think? Good idea/Shit idea?
