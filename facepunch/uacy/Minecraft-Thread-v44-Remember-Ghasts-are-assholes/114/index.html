[QUOTE=Jallen;25816532]Have a ditch or wall (made out of the abundant cobblestone) surrounding your house with a few narrow entry points. So if you decide on a ditch, have the occasional 1-wide bridge over it, and if you decide on a wall, have the occasional 1-wide stair set over it. As long as you keep the entire insides of the walls well lit, you shouldn't have a problem with anything spawning inside. If the creepers do any damage to your ditch or wall, just repair it when you notice it. It's not likely that a creeper will get in a hole made by another one in the same night.
