[QUOTE=daijitsu;25813366]so is the A&#8594;B/B&#8594;C portal thing a glitch?[/QUOTE] It's an issue with how Notch made the portals function. When you make the first portal, it checks in the Nether for the corresponding location. If that location is inside a wall or over lava then it moves it to the nearest safe location. It doesn't check for the original portal when you go back though, it just assumes there's already one in that corresponding location on the main world.
