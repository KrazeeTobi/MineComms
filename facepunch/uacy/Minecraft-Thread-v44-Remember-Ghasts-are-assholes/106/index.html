[QUOTE=desertsniper;25812474]Has anyone figured out how protals work exactly?[/QUOTE] Ask sgt. doom, but I might be able to give you a hand: Portals you make in the real world will only work one way, afaik. Once you go through and come back, the exit portal will be somewhere else. Say you make earth portal [a], and using it creates nether portal [b]. If you return to earth via nether portal [b], you will create a new earth portal [c] relative to the nether position of [b]. Using [a] or [c] will take you to [b], but [b] will only ever take you to [c]. Say you go through [c] or [a] to get to [b], then make a new nether portal [d]. You go through [d] and create earth portal [e]. [e] will be in a position relative to the nether position of [d].
