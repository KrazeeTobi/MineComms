[QUOTE=Inacio;23471965]OK guys, that's it. I'm turning Peaceful off. Any tips[/QUOTE] Going with the meat thing- so many guys complain about sheep and pigs in their base, but mostly in jest. You really do want a huge, more or less level, secure, and lit area so that you get sheep and pig mobs spawning inside your base, because it means you'll never be shy of healing items and some form of armor. Even better if you can throw in a tree farm, because it means you'll also always have wood handy, even at night. You can also use big gobs of trunk and flint to make temporary torches when mining if you haven't hit coal yet.
