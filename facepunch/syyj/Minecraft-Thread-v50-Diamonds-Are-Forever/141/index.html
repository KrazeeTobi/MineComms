[QUOTE=SvendseN;26838664]What exactly is meant by "server-side inventory" ?[/QUOTE] Previously the client managed the inventory and told the server what was happening (which lead to client side exploits and item losses when the server didn't receive the "put item here" message but did receive the "remove item from here" one when moving things) Now the server manages the inventory, and informs the client what's happening. Meaning people can't give themselves items [b]and[/b] items won't get lost while moving them around chests and such.
