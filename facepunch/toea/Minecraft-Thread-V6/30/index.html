Alright guys, for those of you building in my city, I'm adding a new little system to help the city flow better. I've gone through all the buildings and given them a review of sorts.You can see your building's result in the form of colored cubes on the outside of your building. -A red cube means your building is violating the rules and it will be removed unless you fix it. I don't think I gave any reds out today. -A yellow cube means your building needs a quality improvement. This can mean you need to add a more detailed interior or the outside of your building is a little plain. -A green cube means your building simply needs to be higher. If the cube is only 1 block high as opposed to 2, it means you only need to *slightly* improve the quality or make it *slightly* taller. And yes, I did take into account that some buildings aren't complete. <3
