[QUOTE=demonguard;22770102]I'm not entirely sure on the mechanics of obsidian, but could this be used to generate it?[/QUOTE] Nope. Obsidian is only made if water touches a lava source, meaning the full block at the beginning of a stream, and when it does, the block is turned to obsidian and the stream is extinguished, meaning it's a one-time thing unless you have a lot of magma blocks lying around. As a side note, obsidian doesn't turn into cobblestone anymore when mined with a pickaxe (diamond works, not sure about the others) since the last update.
