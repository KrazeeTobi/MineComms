How do I get texture packs to work in Brocraft? Specifically, TTP Lite.
[QUOTE=Oliolio;22773042]How do I get texture packs to work in Brocraft? Specifically, TTP Lite.[/QUOTE] They have to be packaged in .zip format to show up on the drop-down menu when you start the client. If you have WinRAR, right click the files you want to make a .zip and click "Add to Archive". Make sure you select .zip under Archive Format, and hit okay.
