Reposting again.[/QUOTE] Quick google gave me this response: There is a way to move your spawn point in Infdev if you're up for a little work. 1. In Infdev, go stand in the spot you want to set as your new spawn point, then choose Quit, so you 'log out' there. 2. Open up NBTedit (get it from here.) 3. Load your Infdev world, by going to your %appdata%\.minecraft\saves\world1-5\ directory and opening the level.dat file there, in NBTedit. 4. Click the plus sign next to the "Data" folder, where you will see your SpawnX, SpawnY, and SpawnZ stats. This is where the world knows to put you. 5. Change the numbers of these three entries to your current X,Y,Z position, which can be found, in order, under the data>player>pos folder. 6. Save. Not sure how accurate it is, but give it a shot.
