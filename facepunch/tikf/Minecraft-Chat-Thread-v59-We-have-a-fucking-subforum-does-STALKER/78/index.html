Frozen's okay, mobs can't spawn on ice. b) Check for caves underneath the tower and make sure it's well-lit. The more caves the better, in fact it's a great place to start a mine as the more empty space you have under your tower, the better. (SO LONG AS IT'S LIT!) c) Put stone slabs or other such half-blocks on the tower's roof to prevent the buggers from spawning on the top. 5) Make sure the collection point is at least 24 blocks away from the spawning area. This distance works as a radius, so it's a spherical range.
