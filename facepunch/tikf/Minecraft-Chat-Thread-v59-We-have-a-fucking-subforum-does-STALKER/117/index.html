[QUOTE=Mercykiller;28346749]Above.[/QUOTE] Then some suggestions: 1) Dig trenches to plant your trees in, about 5 blocks deep, seperated by a wall with 5 blocks between walls. This is to provide a catwalk so you can more readily access the logs produced by the fuckhueg trees. Staircases or ladders can be used to go to the lower levels. Alternatively, you can simply BUILD catwalks. 2) Make sure critters can't get in. Use either half blocks or replace dirt with stone to prevent animals from spawning inside (they're only a minor nuisance, so this step isn't completely necessary). 3) Make sure the tops of the trees are illuminated. Use either Jack-o-lanterns or Glowstone as leaf blocks will destroy/replace torches when they're grown. This makes sure you don't have hostile mobs spawning on top of your trees. 4) Personal experience shows that planting trees with 4 blocks of space between saplings will provide good tree density without other trees preventing saplings from maturing.
