The RP aspects of our server are as follows -Social Class -Wealth -Barter based Economy -Government -PVE -PvP -Jobs The most important part of our RP system is our government. We are comprised of different groups trying to gain land and resources. So far we have the state of Krabbenstadt, which is based on 18th century Prussia, and Nova Roma, based on the Roman Empire. When you start off, you are part of the Krabbenstadt State, which is completely communist, although some may argue that it is under dictatorship. You will get paid by the state for doing various labor jobs. The city of Krabbenstadt is basically an urban slum, making it fairly undesirable to live in. You can build onto the city whatever you want, as long as it's appropriate during the time period (1800s or before), and fits well with the theme of the city (urban,industrial slum). You may own a modest house in Krabbenstadt, but you must pay a fair amount of resources to the Kaiser(me), to be able to move.
