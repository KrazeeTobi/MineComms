REPOST: [B]Minecraft Beta: December 20, 2010[/B] The next epoch is upon us! The skies are trembling, pigs are growing wings, and the nether is turning awfully cold, as Minecraft slowly crawls out of Alpha.. [B]Minecraft will reach Beta status on December 20th, 2010.[/B] With Beta comes a larger focus on polish and content, and we&#8217;ll start early on with adding proper modding support with a stable API, and we&#8217;d love all input we can get on this from the modders out there. We will also change the license to remove the line that promises all future versions of the game for free. Even better, if you buy those before December 20, accounts created from these codes will count as alpha accounts, and will receive all future updates for free.
