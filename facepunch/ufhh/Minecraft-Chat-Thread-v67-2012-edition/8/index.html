Some things to add to the front page from an old minecrafter: The survival test just tested out very basic mobs, weapons, etc. No crafting. It was a limited world(like 256x256 or something, not sure) Indev was basically the survival test, but with crafting. There was only a few things you could craft, the world was still finite, and mobs were still very.. 'primitive'. Infdev was Indev with an infinite world(With a few added things to the crafting list) Creative wasn't always called Creative. It was just called.. Minecraft, until 'survival mode', or I believe Alpha.. There's your history lesson from what I can remember.
