Flatcore is a pretty neat discovery I found while lurking the mc forums (there's gold in there, but it's damn rare) Basically you start a world with flat terrain and generated structures on hardcore, you then see how long you can survive. You have to basically find a village and tear it into the items you need (it's pretty easy to find one. Usually less than a minute walking) Food is fairly easy to come by via farms, but higher tech stuff (and saplings) are harder to come by, you need to loot blacksmiths. Pretty fun and interesting, good way to mix it up. The LUDICROUS number of slimes may fuck you over though.
