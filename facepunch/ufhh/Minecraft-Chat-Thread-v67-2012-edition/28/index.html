I don't get how last page everyone was saying that zombies shouldnt have the path finding, or that all the mobs should. Its the zombies advantage, think about it. Skeletons can shoot, spiders can climb, creepers can explode, what can zombies do? Out of the 4 basic mobs in the game, zombies do nothing. You get attacked by a horde of skeletons? Thats a problem. 2 spiders attack you? Bit of an issue. A single creeper? Thats hard to deal with. I've seen people stop dead in their tracks and work the best way [I]around[/I] a creeper to make sure it doesn't notice them. Yet the zombies you can just mow down with ease. The fact they have advanced path finding should be their thing...and not any other mobs thing. Maybe update the current mobs path finding but they should always keep zombies as really smart. And to those who say that zombies are dumb and shouldn't be able to track you so easily...man just say they have a nose for human flesh or something. It fits guys srsly.
The other mobs do sorta need better path-finding though. I stopped a Creeper by walking away from it, placing a 2 block high wall in front of it, then peeking around the corner and sealing him in a box of snow blocks. He was too dumb to just walk around the 2x1x1 pillar I constructed in front of him.
The Zombie pathfinding still has a lot of flaws though. When they walk on Soul Sand or Slabs, they spin in place like an undead Catherine wheel, and they seem almost too happy to walk straight through burning Netherrack. Still, they're not as bad as the Testificates.
