I found a map on youtube i think, where something with the generation of the world had gone wrong. It had spawned a massive village, stretching some 300 blocks. And that gave me an idea. For those who dont know, last days puts you in a post-apocalyptic world where you have to scavange for food and materials. The map, combined with the pack gave a pretty amazing experience. The houses had overlapped eachother, creating all sorts of strange shacks and shelters. Here are some pictures of my explorations: A pile of houses.
