[img]http://www.minecraft.net/img/logo.gif[/img] [i]Thanks to to the person that renamed the thread. Sorry for that guys.[/i] MineCraft, is game made by Markus Persson. It was started on the 10th May 09. Its a 3d block based game created in 3d and in Java, meaning it is [b]browser based[/b]. It is free at this stage but for custom skins and the new game modes (not released yet) you will have to pay a fee of 10 euros, this price will rise further into development. The game is similar to the likes of Infiniminer. You can place and destroy blocks. It has many features such as: fluids, gravity and the map generator is very good. If that is not enough, read this: [b]Single Player[/b] You click singleplayer on the front page and the level is generated, you have deep seas, sandy beaches and rolling hills. Huge cave complex's and beneath your feet, ready for exploration. It auto generates forests and other features. As yo wander the many valleys, you wonder, where are the houses? You right click and band you can now place blocks, press B and you change what block to use! And you can set all the blocks from 1-9, then use the keys to select each one. You build yourself using glass, wood and grand floors. Fun? [b]Multiplayer[/b] This is where the fun kicks in, you join up on the server. Bang in front of you are people all around, grand structures being built. Some of it is truly awe inspiring, you get lost in the maze of buildings, murals and towers. Want to build a megastructure? Now you can do it with friends, meaning you can make it twice as epic in the same amount of time it would of taken you alone.
