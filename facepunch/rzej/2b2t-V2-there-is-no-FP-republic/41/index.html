We also met back up with chance and gave him some of our loot. Kstalawl came running back through the area a few times. The first time he came back, we chased him around with diamond swords until on a rainy night we had him up against a corner in an old cobblestone house. We made fun of him and asked him some questions. He gave us details on his base, told us how he didn't know parcec, and said that he raided our base because we were too close to his. After a while, we all agreed that parcec wasn’t coming back any time soon, and camping there was a complete waste. We went thousands of blocks away, and at one point, godilla went to sleep. We went another few thousand blocks out and made a small house in a cave. At this point, parcec started messaging us, and made up a story of how kstalawl and original_ganker were intruders to his base. Me and chaos believed this, though toothycat and godilla101 were suspicious from the start. He came over to our house, and we talked a bit and logged off. We continued on to make a new base at bedrock, which we stayed at for 4 days. Over this time, we noticed a stack of diamond and logs missing. There was also some other guy in the area who messed with our nether portal in a cave nearby and placed signs about how he is going to find our base in an ice ocean nearby. His name was animefan9. A few days passed, everyone got bored and went off to play some other games. After playing an hour or two of TF2, toothycat logged back in to find that our entire base was gone. I went down and found kstalawl, and we also killed some other guy, his name was something dictator. I searched through what was once our base, and found a nice poem: ROSES ARE RED VIOLETS ARE BLUE DON’T FUCK WITH ME SIGNED, FUNNYBUNNY I went back upstairs and saw enigma937. I killed him, and holyshiz logged on since he had our only diamond left. He was running around when he casually told us that someone was shooting him with arrows. That guy turned out to be parcec. We all went back downstairs to find him still there. He vanished, and while I was trying to find this tunnel he told me about, chaoslink and toothycat were telling me to just get the hell out of there, because we assumed the coords were leaked, since we didn't know who all these other inruders were.
