Just tried joining for the first time. This is amazing! 1. Died a few times while trying to escape the massive walls of water at spawn. My netbook lags pretty hard in Minecraft and my view distance is minimal, which makes navigating over the huge, random pits a little tricky. 2. Finally manage to get out of spawn! Someone was kind enough to leave a small tunnel going straight through the waterfalls. 3. While randomly wandering the lands, darkness suddenly falls. What the hell? That was way too fast to be sunset. I turn around and realize that the land behind me is still bright. Then I realize: there's a massive platform in the sky blocking the sun from all the land in front of me (my view distance was too small to see it). I set about collecting two stacks of dirt to make the climb. 4. An epic escape! As soon as I finish collecting dirt a gang of skeletons converge on me (they must have been spawning in the darkness of the platform)! I frantically build upwards as arrows lodge themselves in my little tower. The platform is HUGE! Once again, because of my draw distance I can't see the limit of it. After walking on it for a while (and wondering how the hell I'll ever get down) it conveniently connects with the top of a grand tower. 5. The tower has a portal to the Nether! Off I go! The fortifications in the Nether are impressive: huge walkways spanning the lava seas, massive staircases leading off into the distance. There are hundreds of furnaces everywhere, which leads me to think that the builders used a duplication bug in order to gather these resources. After walking for quite a while I find another portal. I take it, with the hope of emerging far from the hell of spawn. 6. The portal exit is inside a natural cave. Multiple pathways lit by torches twist through the rock and I follow them at random. I eventually find myself in the belly of a massive underground base (former FP property?). I lost track of how many massive rooms I walk by (impressively, I even find grass). The base is deserted and appears to be thoroughly looted, judging by the sparse torch placement. 7. I'd love to stay and look around but I can hear zombies all around me - they've taken up residence now that the torches are gone. A single massive staircase seems promising and I follow it, though it quickly plunges into complete darkness. I run on, fearing that mobs will start spawning within my only hope of escape. Eventually I hit a dead end, but I can see the edges of grass on the dirt blocks. 8. I clear away the dirt. Shit: it's night and I can hear spiders. Luckily I'm on a small ledge on a cliff. I stick my single torch (which I scavenged from the underground base) against the wall and try to wall off a bit using my leftover dirt. There are four spiders with their eyes on me: one above and three below. One begins to climb, I swing and miss, he takes a bite out of my health and knocks me backwards... off of my ledge. I land in the waiting swarm below and die. 9. I'm back at spawn and get my first sign of human life! I knight in glittering armor named dbz933 stands beside me! As I make small talk he punches me to death repeatedly. I lose my connection with the server.
