[IMG]http://imgur.com/HjyiOID.png[/IMG] 2b2t is the longest running and largest minecraft world ever made. There are no rules, no staff, and infinite possibility, in a completely vanilla setting. The world (at the time of writing this) is 787GB large and 49 months old (19 Jan 2015) and nearly 60,000 players have wandered the world at least once. The server will continue to grow and age as long as the community pays $90 monthy to maintain it. If you're someone who loves to explore, discover and create this is the server for you, there are a multitude of bases, forms of terrain to explore, and (thanks to a few dozen exploits, and even one back-door over the years) plenty of OP gear, loot, and resources to steal, provided you're lucky and persistent enough to go out there and find it. At peak times the server has around thirty players on, some are friendly, most are deadly. Facepunch was also part of its history, as proven on this threads: [URL="http://facepunch.com/showthread.php?t=1083343"]Here[/URL], [URL="http://www.facepunch.com/showthread.php?t=1105156"]Here[/URL] [URL="http://facepunch.com/showthread.php?t=1160481"]Here[/URL], [URL="http://facepunch.com/showthread.php?t=1174308"]Here[/URL], [URL="http://facepunch.com/showthread.php?t=1250221&page=33"]Here[/URL] and [URL="http://facepunch.com/showthread.php?t=1418046"]Here[/URL]. In 27th April of 2011, Chezhead attempted to make a Facepunch Republic in the server, however he startled most of the 4chan playerbase in the process. Because of this, the Republic never happened, being sabotaged over and over again by infiltrated players and griefers. But things have changed... 2b2t is currently in a era of peace. Griefers and griefings have decreased in number through the years. Because of this, bases like Valkyria, Gape, Asgard, Nova and Fenrir were able to happen. Playing in 2b2t is not just playing minecraft. This server is the ultimate minecraft online experience. No rules. No Admins. Welcome to Minecraft Hell.
