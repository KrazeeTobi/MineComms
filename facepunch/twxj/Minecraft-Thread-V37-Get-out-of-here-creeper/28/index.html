How would I go about making a pick?[/QUOTE] After you make a crafting square, place it and right click it. The recipe is as follows: [code]W W W A S A A S A [/code] A is Air, S is a stick, W is wood. Once you get some cobblestone, you can substitute that for the wood, and if you get some iron, you can put that in place of the cobblestone, and diamond replaces iron.
