You have until nightfall to build yourself a little shelter to protect yourself from the nighttime monsters. Spoiler if you want to try and figure it out by yourself, if you just want to know how to survive your first minecraft night, read this: [sp]You should cut down some trees. Drop all the tree blocks into your crafting area (press i to open your inventory) to create planks. Take all your new planks and drop them back on the crafting area, this time one in each of the 4 blocks. This creates a workbench that allows you to craft things on a 3x3 area. Move this out into your inventory, possibly on the very bottom so you can place it later. Right click on the planks that remain to grab half of them. Put them on any block in the crafting area. Grab the other half, and put them above or below the first one (depending on where you put it). This should generate sticks. In order to survive the night, you need a light source, because monsters can pop out from anywhere that's dark. But you can't make light sources without coal. Place your workbench somewhere on the ground. If you have any planks remaining, use them, otherwise go grab 2 or 3 more tree trunks and turn them into planks. In order to mine coal, you need a pickaxe, so right click the workbench to craft a wooden pickaxe. Drop two sticks on the bottom and middle center blocks of the 3x3 grid, then put planks over the entire top row. You should have a pickaxe. Now without straying too far from your spawn area, scan the area to see if you can find any gray rock. If you can, look around and see if there's any rock with little black spots on it. That's coal. Go ahead and mine it with your pickaxe, once you've got one or two, press i to get to the inventory and place one stick down and a piece of coal just over that in the crafting area. Congratulations, you have 4 torches. Break your workbench (don't worry, it reappears in your inventory) Mine out a little home into the side of a hill or something, and cover the entrance up, leaving just a 1x1 hole to see what time of day it is. Place the workbench and a torch there. Wait out the first night. If you're feeling a little bold, go mining at night, but don't forget your torches and backup pickaxes.[/sp]
