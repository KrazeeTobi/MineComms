[QUOTE=Archy;25238420]you know what minecraft needs sloped tiles[/QUOTE] It does. Even if it's just 45 degree angles (which are already programmed in perfectly in the form of stair blocks), it wouldn't really hurt the style THAT much to have a slanted block type. But there is a problem with slanted blocks, and Notch has addressed why he's not putting them in: They are far too hard to manage placing-wise. The sloped block would have to face you when you place it, and Notch said that'd require a lot of coding to get right. As it is now, stairs face you when you place them UNLESS there is a block on all sides except one, then it will face that way, and that just wouldn't be practical for building. Furnaces show this sometimes with the open end being off in some weird direction you didn't place it in. Long story short, Notch thinks the idea of sloped tiles wouldn't be too bad, they're just a bitch to code the way everyone would expect them to work.
