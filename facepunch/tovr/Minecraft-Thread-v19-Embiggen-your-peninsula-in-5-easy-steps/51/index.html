Your local computer's IP address. Start>run, cmd, ipconfig /all. [img]http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/6/6c/Ipconfig_win_xp.png[/img] You are looking for "Ethernet" and probably "Local Area Connection" (but sometimes your network adapter may have a different name), and the "IP Address" line. As your router states, it'll begin 192.168.1. The default port to forward is 25565 but that's configurable in the settings file so just use the same number in both.
