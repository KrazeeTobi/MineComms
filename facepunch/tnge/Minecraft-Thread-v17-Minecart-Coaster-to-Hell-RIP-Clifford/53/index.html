So I thought I lucked out when I generated my newest world. Immediately when I spawned I saw a cave entrance neatly staring at me, and so I dove in head first. Of course, it was dark as fuck. Luckily there was a lot of coal near by. My second venture into the cave with around 60 torches was much better. I created some ladders just in case, and armed with my torches I was ready. I was not however, ready for this: [IMG]http://i21.photobucket.com/albums/b268/JaxStrife/mc1.png[/IMG] This picture was taken after I had done my exploring, and decided to share my cave. I descended the cave and found that it branched out into two different tunnels. One of these tunnels lead to HFS. [IMG]http://i21.photobucket.com/albums/b268/JaxStrife/mc2.png[/IMG] The other tunnel eventually branched out again and ended with a natural spring. I was constantly running around planting torches just to find alternate paths, which ultimately lead under the ocean. I plan on doing something amazing with this cave system, but I'm not exactly sure what. I do plan on making a spiral cart road for the initial drop though. Also, from the chests I got two saddles, lots of iron, wheat, bread, and a few arrows.
I cheated my rollercoaster cuz my chest bugged and ate all my iron. I feel bad for it though :(.
