Monorails can solve that.i imagine them to be sort of like minecarts, but upside down. The recipe for one would be a furnace, but with iron instead of cobblestone. The type could also be specified by adding a chest in the middle space, for cargo, or leaving it as be for passengers.Below is a crudely drawn diagram of the idea. As you can see, monorails would be useful for quick transportation between towns or mining facilities. &#8220;But wait,&#8221; you may say, &#8220;what would happen if the monorail car,&#8221; hereby referred to as the car, &#8220; was derailed?&#8221;. Either A: Fiery disaster or B: The car would fall of the tracks continueing at the speed it was already going at, killing mobs and destroying blocks until coming to a stop. Getting out would be impossible until it stopped, and you would steadily take damage. It coulld also turn while flying through the air, breaking at impact if going fast enough. When it breaks apart, the car becomes the inventory item or shatters back into iron ingots. Unrealistic, sure, but so is shoving a minecart into your pants. In between the paragraphs are diagrams.
