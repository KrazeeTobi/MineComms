Depends if it's connected to grass, on the best of my understanding.
Grass will not grow on dirt tiles that are too far from any existing grass tile. They must be be adjacent and no higher than one block above (so technically you could have two dirt blocks stacked next to a grass tile and still have the grass grow on top) the grass tile. If you want grass to reach up there, the easiest course of action would be to construct a dirt staircase and let the grass slowly inch its way up.
