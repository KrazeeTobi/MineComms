They drop powder which can be used to make TNT along with sand. [i]Skeletons[/i] are the walking bones you see, and they shoot arrows at you. Just strafe them and land a couple hits with your sword on them. They drop arrows. [i]Spiders[/i] are the bane of every miner's existance. They jump really high and can usually outrun you. They are wide, however, so they have trouble getting through doorways. These drop string so you can make bows. [i]Zombies[/i] are really not much of a threat, but can attack you from above and kill you. They drop feathers for whatever reason which are used to make arrows. [i]Slime[/i] are the smallest mob when in baby form. Baby slimes don't grow in Peaceful mode, but in anything above, they can grow to be 5x5+ big. They're friendly in peaceful, but when above baby form they can attack. [i]Pigs[/i] are friendly creatures which give you meat when you kill them, and if you find a sattle, you can "ride" them. [i]Sheep[/i] are friendly and will drop wool/cloth, which is mostly useless. [i]Cows[/i] drop leather when killed, and leather is used to make armor. They also double as musical instruments. [i]Chickens[/i] drop eggs from time to time, and when killed, they sometimes drop feathers. They are the second smallest mob.
