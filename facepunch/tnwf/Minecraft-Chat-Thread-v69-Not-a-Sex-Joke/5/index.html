[QUOTE=marcosppp;37824022]I really want to know how to not make a box house. I don't control it anymore. My brain just likes boxes.[/QUOTE] Here's a few tips: I. Go on servers and walk around a bit. Get into the habit of taking pictures of houses that you like. II. For my best designs, I usually build a house in a singleplayer world with Creative mode, then transplant it into a Survival world. Open up your screenshots and take a look at the photos you got earlier. III. There are a lot of houses that use pretty simple geometry, but with different materials. If you're building a wooden house, switch up the materials. Add some logs to break up the monotony. If you have space for it, add some fences and place torches on top to give a nice bracket look to it.
