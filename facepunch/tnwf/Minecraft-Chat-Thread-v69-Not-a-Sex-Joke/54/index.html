Minecraft just isn't the right place to bring that can of worms, in my opinion. I mean, I wouldn't use a magic mod like that since I don't see it as necessary anyway - I quite like the physicality of Minecraft, the focus on materials and objects rather than abstract concepts like morality or magic or the human psyche makes it inherently a simple, soothing game to play. I disagree, I think it'd work well if it was done right. Positive magic would be based around healing, tending to crops/passive animals, and reducing damage done to yourself, your mining speed and other things that magic would be useful for in an enviroment where monsters don't spawn, while negative magic would be based around doing more damage, and this is where you'd get your necromancy, your fire wizardry, your ability to absolutely and totally dominate everything around you no matter what's destroyed in the process. Good and bad would be concepts to describe defensive and offensive, respectively, rather than [I]actually[/I] being good or bad.
