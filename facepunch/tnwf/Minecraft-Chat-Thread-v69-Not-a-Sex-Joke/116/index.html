I remember my first world. I had not seen a single lets-play or tutorial and was basically thrown into the game with no knowledge whatsoever. I had however played classic, so i was not totally lost with the basic mechanics of the game. I spawned in a mountain range, climbed around a bit until i found the entrance to a cave. (May i remind you i had no idea what monsters were, or what happens at night.) I was heading down into the cave when i suddenly caught sight of something in the dark. A skinny man stood at the bottom and looked back up on me. A loud TWANG and half a heart later i was running away from the mountains hoping the skinny man was not following me. I found a beach and a couple of tiny islands situated some 30 blocks out in the water. I thought that was a good place to build a house, so i went out there. After trying to click the dirt blocks away for 5 minutes i eventually understood you had to hold the mousebutton down in order to dig. So i built myself a dirt hut, and before i knew it, night fell. Still scared from my encounter with the skinny cave man, the night was just as frightening. On the shore i saw strange creatures jumping around in the sand, including what i thought was a red-eyed man crawling on all four. I stopped playing, and didn't play again a week later when i had watched some basic tutorials. Im actually really glad i tried it out without knowing anything about it. I can look back at that time and think, "Man, what a dumbass". That was sometime before the Halloween update containing nether and biomes.
