TFC is a realism conversion mod, so if you don't like having realistic-ish blacksmithing, geology, farming and stuff like that it's probably not for you. Once you find metal and make your first tool stuff gets a bit more interesting, but being stuck in the stone age is hell. The mod progresses a ton faster in multiplayer, and it's more fun with friends certainly.
