Are most ages like that? Does it have something to do with me creating them so fast?[/QUOTE] [i]Any[/i] age you don't write yourself will be unstable. I forget the exact categories you need to fill out, but I think you need to write an age with 1 biome definer (small biomes, etc.) a number of biomes depending on the biome definer (1 for single, 2 for checker, 3+ for anything else), a time symbol, lighting symbol, and weather symbol. Everything else can be automatically filled, but if any of the above are automatically filled by the game, it'll be unstable. Some symbols come with instability too. I haven't looked at Mystcraft in a little while but from what I've seen now you get symbols by buying them from Mystcraft villagers. Make a notebook with 9 paper and look for villages.
You get symbols by making ages and putting the link books on a writing desk with a notebook on it.
