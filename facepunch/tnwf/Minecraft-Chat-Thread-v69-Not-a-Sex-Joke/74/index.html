[QUOTE=Jallen;38590470]Ah ok. I've learned tekkit almost entirely from yogscast so I haven't seen them. What's better about them?[/QUOTE] Pneumatic tubes (redpower ones) are smart about moving items. Unlike BC pipes, which dump items into the pipes and let the items take random routes through the pipe, pneumatic tubes will send items through into the nearest inventory that can accept items. An example would be putting items into several furnaces. With BC, pumping a bunch of different ores into the furnaces would make some land in the furnaces, and drop the rest onto the ground since it won't fit. With RP, if the item won't fit into the nearest inventory, the tube sends the item along to the next available inventory instead of dropping it on the ground.

They'll automatically collect and spit items that fall on top of the face into pneumatic tubes, and if you give it a redstone signal it'll either pull an item out of a chest or collect every item on the ground in a 3x3 area on the face.
