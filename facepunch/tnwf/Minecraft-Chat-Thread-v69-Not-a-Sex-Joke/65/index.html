[QUOTE=RockmanYoshi;38416501]So what exactly does terrafirmacraft do?[/QUOTE] TFC is an overhaul mod. Instead of adding new things to the game it completely changes the game. I can't really cover everything but there's a lot more depth and challenge to it. You have to pick up wrocks, smash them together into tool heads, break leaves to get sticks and craft the head and stick together to make tools. You can't make stone tools either, so you can't break stone for a long time (You have to make a metal pick, which means you have to slowly get small ore nuggets off the ground until you can amass one measily ingot to make a pick with). There's a lot of other changes (The land generator is a lot prettier, and there's more trees and stuff), you kind of just have to play it. I watch Spumwuck, and he makes tutorials/letsplays for the mod, I'd suggest going to him. He explains it very well.
