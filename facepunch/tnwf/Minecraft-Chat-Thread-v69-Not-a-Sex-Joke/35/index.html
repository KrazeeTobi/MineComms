Guys, i have a very strange story to share with you. Now, first of all, yes. This is a story starring a strange happenings in minecraft. No, i dont think its Herobrine, but it is most certainly... something. My friend started up a survival server earlier today for us to play on. We thought we would try the newest snapshot so all of us installed it and joined the server. It all started out fairly basic, we teamed up in pairs and started making plans of our future. Fortresses, farms and the like. Me and my friend was exploring this cave we found on the surface wich led down and into a ravine. So we stuck around for a while and mined what few ores we could find on such a high altitude of the underground. We were mining a vein of coal when suddenly a strange sound caught our attention. It started out as a low static, but within seconds got higher and a low moaning sound could be heard. Me and my friend started freaking out when the moaning got higher and it almost sounded like a scream was building up. So we ran the fuck up to the surface, scared shitless. Now, ive played minecraft for almost two years now and i have never ever heard anything like that. It was most certainly not an ambience sound because as i turned and ran, i could hear the surround of the sound. It was very close to us, possibly around the corner or on the other side of the stone. That was our first encounter with this strange phenomenon that we eventually started calling "The groke", named after the big black ghost from Moomin. The second encounter was on the surface, in daylight. I was running past my friends house when the static started. I stopped, listened this time for a source. "Hey! Get over here, its back!" I said to my freind. He got there as quick as possible, but the only sound he heard was a faint shhhhh that faded out. Now we had three witnesses of this "Groke" so we started trying to figure out what the sound was. The fact it had surround means it was not ambience but something coming from a source. We went through the resource folder in .minecraft but found nothing unusual. This simply made no sense. We were all three certain that this was real, it was not our mind playing tricks. Needless to say, this server has got a lot scarier now. Noone dares to mine alone and there is this constant feeling of dread looming over all of us. I am not trying to be dramatic, and im not making this up. Wether it was something Mojang implemented as a secret for Halloween, or if our minds really are playing tricks on us, it scared us out of our senses.
