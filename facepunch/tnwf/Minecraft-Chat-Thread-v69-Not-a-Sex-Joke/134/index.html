Honestly, if you don't want something as cluttered as FTB, I'd recommend a setup somewhat like: Forge, Optifine in the JAR CodeChickenCore and NEI in coremods Redpower2 (all modules), Thaumcraft 3, ExtraBiomesXL, Buildcraft, Railcraft, Forestry, Thermal Expansion, your choice of Rei's or Zan's minimap (recommend Zan's), maybe ExtraBees and Thaumic Bees in mods That's a very well-rounded set of mods that [i]in my opinion[/i] provides a similar experience to a larger modpack without being quite so cluttered or full of confusing duplicate functionality or game-breakingly powerful gear like the Quantum Suit. The main omission from this set compared to other modpacks is IndustrialCraft 2. A lot of IC2 functionality is replicated in Redpower2 and Thermal Expansion, but with more interesting mechanics and/or more polish. Including IC2 as well just gives you a third power system to work with (Blutricity, Buildcraft Mechanical Power and IC2 EU systems) and a hell of a lot of unpolished looking stuff with overly complex and rarely sensible crafting recipes. I don't miss it at all. But, if you also don't want to do custom mod installs (which, yes, can be a pain; I recommend [url=http://forkk.net/MultiMC4]MultiMC[/url] as usual for these, managing mods in your Minecraft instances is extremely easy and I've never encountered any issues with its automated mod installing) then booting up an FTB pack isn't really all that scary. Just remember: NEI is your friend. Need to know how to craft something? Search for it by name, R for recipes. Wondering what the fuck this thing you picked up is for? U for usage. Those two keys alone will save you a thousand wiki trips - in fact, I managed to progress through the tech tree of the Direwolf20 pack from start to finish without checking wikis at all, simply by using NEI for recipes and experimenting to see how things work. Many explosions were had, and it was actually really fun because I was in the game and focused on it and not really worrying about doing things "right". Could not recommend doing this more :V
