Personally I'd like to see a proper stamina bar and have hunger tied in with regeneration and events as well as ticks. Sprinting consumes about 10% of your stamina per second, jumping consumes about 10% (yes this would severely limit mobility in mountains) Regenerating stamina costs hunger (about 1 unit of hunger per 2 bars of stamina), as does regenerating health (1 unit of hunger per 4 units of health), mining or placing blocks consumes a tiny bit, and it gradually declines passively (very, very slowly - should take about 2 minutes to lose one full point of hunger if you're just walking around not doing much - about 40 minutes to a hunger bar) At full hunger you have 100% stamina and health regen. Your health regen slows by 10% every unit of hunger, so 10 units of hunger means you no longer regenerate health. Then your stamina regen starts going at the same speed for the remaining 10 units, and at 0 hunger you no longer regenerate health or stamina. If you're out of stamina at 0 hunger, you start taking damage, so if you play your cards right and don't need to sprint or jump you can survive starvation.
