To all the ignorant folks who are saying, "HURR DURR I CANT MAKK MOAT DIS SUX," or, "HURR DURR I CANT MAKK RIVER DIS SUX," you can make a moat or river, and it can be more than one block deep. In fact, Notch even tells you how to do so in his blog post. And for those who are too lazy to go to the blog themselves, I am just going to cut out the important part and put it here. [quote]Water will not spread out on top of existing water, but it won't drop down if you dig below it. So if you want to make a river several blocks deep, first make one one block deep, then dig below each water block.[/quote] If you can't comprehend this, what he is saying is that water won't go on [B][U]top[/U][/B] of other blocks of water but it will go [B][U]under[/U][/B] other blocks. So the simple solution is to start with a one block deep river or moat, then put water in it by use of a bucket while it is still one block deep, and the dig the moat deeper while the water is in it. Then eureka, you have a moat or a river that is as deep as you want.
