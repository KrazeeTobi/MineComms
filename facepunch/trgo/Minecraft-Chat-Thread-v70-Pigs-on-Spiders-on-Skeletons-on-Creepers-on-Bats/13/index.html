[t]http://i.imgur.com/HMlvOfm.jpg[/t] Yes, it's a series of Steve's Carts. But, and here's the trick, they're all connected using [I]Railcraft's[/I] cart linking system. The carts on the ends have a drill, a liquid sensor, an advanced shooter set to kill monsters, a bridge builder, a track layer, and a torch placer. All the other carts besides the workbench cart are just full up on storage, with seats every two carts because that's how far apart you can still interact with them while seated. It's pretty fucking baller. One issue is that all those modules only work if the cart is powered by an engine, and the only engines move the cart. I'mma suggest engines that don't cause the cart to move, or a way to decouple the transmission or something, so that you can have active carts in places besides the ends
