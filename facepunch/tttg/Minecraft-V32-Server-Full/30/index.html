[url]http://minecraft.net/servers.jsp[/url] Remember that that's the creative, sandbox version of the game. It doesn't have the monsters, new blocks/items, fancy lighting or water physics, new level generator, or almost infinite terrain. In creative you can basically just place and remove blocks, which you are given an infinite supply of. There's modded creative servers too, which usually have stuff like ranks, the ability to place water and lava, and other things.
