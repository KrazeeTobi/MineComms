The weird part however, is that torches that are in the outer fringes of the caves seem to be disappearing. The torches near the staircase I have built down are never touched, but once I explore an area and return my haul to the surface and make my return trip to explore more, my torches seem to be gone in certain areas. The weirdest part of this is that it is not random, torches go missing in big clumps so that I am forced to walk through the dark to place more. I know it is not just my imagination because I always place torches near mineral patches like iron or coal so I can get it later, and I have had torches go missing from those areas. I decided to do an experiment, to see if maybe some mystery mob or other secret update shenanigans were going on, and I placed a cube of glass around my torches in a long line down a fringe tunnel. I returned to the surface and started chopping down trees to kill time, and because I needed wood badly. I went down to the surface, using the only armor bits I had, made of cloth. I also got an iron sword just in case. I had some meat, but not much. As I made my way down into the cave, I was surprised to see that all of the torches that were encased in glass survived what ever had been taking the others. I noticed a glass box, with no torch in it about halfway down the line, and I saw that I had missed a side of the glass box, exposing the torch. Something had taken this torch, the experiment had confirmed it. At this point I was excited and scared at the same time, since I had never seen anything like this happen in minecraft before.
