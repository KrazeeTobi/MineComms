Miners log 002: Still in my personal mine. I retreated from my mine for the night. Those beasts won't get to me if I stay in my small house for the night. And it will give me time to heal. I'll make a weapon and go hunting for a day. Anything to clear my mind of that horror. I find a field of pigs, and go to town! Pork fills my backpack. I return home to grill this into some tastier, and less dripping with blood. Night time already, I head back to my mine. returning to the open caverns I discovered. I still hear that groaning, what is down here? I zero in one what I think is the source of it, but there is a wall in my way. After piecing together a simple pickaxe I dig my way through. But what do I find on the other side? Mossy cobblestone. I dig through this slightly so that I can look into the room it contains, I see a horde of zombies, and the source of my problems! And mob spawner just waiting to be broken. I clear out the zombies that are near my hole with my sword, that extra reach you gain from a few pounds of iron really helps. I climb into the room, and quickly look in a chest nearby the spawner, I find some iron ingots, two saddles, a bucket (oh yay, more buckets), and some thread. I destroy the mob spawner, and retreat back into my mine. Happy that I can be safe again. As I try to leave I get shot down by a small grouping of Skeletons, fuck this shit. ---------- Yeah I got lazy with this one. But that sums up my mining experiences thus far.
