Had a bit of an idea for Redstone again. By arranging 4 Redstone Dust in a 2x2 box, one could create a Redsand block, which falls like Sand, and carries a Redstone current like regular Redstone. If used right, it could act as a circuit breaker/connector (it could fall from the ceiling onto a circuit gap, or fall out of a circuit if certain conditions are met) or a vertical "charge carrier" (it could carry the current to the block above it). And then there's the other application, which is smelting it in a Furnace to make a Redstone Ruby, which'd be useful for all sorts of cool stuff like mechanisms or even Tools/Armour.
