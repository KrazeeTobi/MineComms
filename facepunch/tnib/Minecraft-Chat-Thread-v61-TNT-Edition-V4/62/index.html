I still think that "Sulphur" and Coal should be craftable into Blackpowder, which is basically gunpowder. But since guns are out of the question (no-brainer), one could always make bombs with them. The basic Bomb would have the Blackpowder at the centre, a piece of String in the top-right square, and 4 Flint in the top, bottom, left and right squares. The end-result is basically a throwable explosive that looks like the traditional cartoon bomb. It isn't stackable, so don't expect to be able to Demospam. It doesn't destroy as much of the environment as a TNT block, as it has an explosive power of 2, as opposed to the 4 of TNT, the 3 of the Creeper, and the 1 of the Ghast's Fireball. For more info on Explosions, check the wiki page. [URL]http://www.minecraftwiki.net/wiki/Explosion[/URL] If you were able to afford it, you could in theory exchange the Flint for Iron to yield a more powerful bomb, and if you were really REALLY rich, one could even use Diamond for a bomb of maximum quality, greater even than TNT and the Powder Keg (i'll explain later). Although I wouldn't necessarily encourage such usage of Diamonds, even if it looked awesome in that deleted scene from The Last Minecart. Best to stick with Iron if you can afford it. Add 3 Slimeballs to the crafting recipe (top-left, bottom-left, bottom-right) to create a Sticky Bomb, capable of sticking to surfaces and mobs, for more "predictable" demolitions. Another potential recipe using Blackpowder could be the Powder Keg, using 3 Blackpowder and 6 Planks to make a Powder Keg, a more expensive, stabler and more powerful version of TNT (Kegs have a Power rating of 5). Hitting a Keg won't set it off; you'll need to set it on fire, but you can't ignite it just like that since it'll blow up in your face. This is where another quality of Blackpowder comes in; it is both flammable and placeable, similar to Redstone Dust. Simply leave a trail of Blackpowder leading away from the Keg, light the fuse, and watch it blow. Basically, I propose advanced Gunpowder, Bombs, Sticky Bombs that use Slimeballs, and a blackpowder keg that is more powerful, more complex and more expensive than TNT.
