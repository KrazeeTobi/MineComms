  • Deepforge, possibly the longest standing 2b2t settlement.
    32 replies, posted
Deepforge, starting late in July, several citizens of Boatmurdered departed their fair city to start anew, further out in the wilderness. They traveled for hours until reaching land they could call home. What began as the town of Forge, so named for the initial use of massive amounts of furnaces to mold our stone buildings, eventually thrived. Some left, others arrived. Names such as PoliceMike55, Popbob, McShmeggy, Mindule, Wilko, and KiwiWololo came and went. Eventually we broke into a ravine rich in minerals and space. At my behest we began to transition ourselves back below the surface, as we once had done in Boatmurdered. We were dwarves at heart with heart that yearned for the stone. And so we dug, and molded the ravines to our will, we tiered them thrice, we allotted space for all who may come. But, over the time, few came. and fewer stayed. The months rolled by and eventually only two stayed. Myself, and Kiwi. So updates came and went, we began to emerge from our depths, for the first time in nearly 4 months I say that square sun, in that awful blue sky. And I began to build a tower to the heavens from which I could see all who may approach. It was built, and it was good. A garden atop it, stone from hell itself adorning it while my mechanized golems patrolled it's heights. It was perfect, but also lonely. So I decided to forsake the rituals of invitation and spam the coordinates among the highest winds for all to hear and all to come. But before that I would document what I had wrought here. What with the hands of several men built longingly, and kept safe for so long. Here lies Deepfore July 28th 2011-March 30th 2012 pictures below are from the Sky-garden.
