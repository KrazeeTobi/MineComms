[QUOTE=Tsanummy;26178366]Hey, I bought this game today and I'm trying it. I've some questions about the game, can I regenerate life? Can I save items or if I die I lose everything? How do you build those huge houses, I mean, if you die, don't you respawn in the first respawn and you've to walk again all the way? Can you save your respawn point? Where do I find light, glasses, etc, so far I only found stone, wood and sand, also, it's boring to wait all the night buried in the terrain not being able to do anything... Hope I'm not a pain in the neck for you guys[/QUOTE] Well, life regenerates on peaceful difficulty. But if you want enemy mobs (monsters), then you have to play on Easy or higher. You can restore some HP by eating. Get porks by killing pigs, cooked porks by smelting them in a furance (heals more), fish, bread and so on. When you die, you respawn on your default, not-movable spawn point. (Well technically you can move it through a map editor) You also drop all the items in your inventory at death. They will stay at the point where you died for about 5 minutes, so if you want your stuff back, you will have to hurry. Houses, well, the best thing to do is hide at night, build at day. Keep in mind that holding SHIFT will prevent you from falling off edges. For light, use torches; you just have to craft wooden sticks and coal. Check out wiki for all the recipes. Also useful is lightstone, but that's only found in hell. Glass is made by smelting sand in a furnance. Again, rest of the recipes are on wiki. [url]http://www.minecraftwiki.net/wiki/Main_Page[/url]
