My most effective way to mine is simply to create a mine, as in a small building with stairs leading down, and a that is eight or so blocks wide and long. I just dig the first layer of 64 blocks (minus one for stairs) and have a pretty even amount of cobblestone for building. Once you have dug your way to the bottom, you should have enough stone to make a small city, as I do. That's when the goodies start rolling in. Just make another passage just a couple blocks above the bedrock going straight into the side of the mine, about 4 blocks wide and 2 or 3 high, depending on how much stone you still need. This provides a nice little stockpile of ores and such.
