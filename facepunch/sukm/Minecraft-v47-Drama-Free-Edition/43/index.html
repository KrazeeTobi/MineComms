[QUOTE=Mikesword221;26132490]Guys, how do I apply AO to my skin?[/QUOTE] Darken spots on your skin where light wont reach it.
[QUOTE=Mikesword221;26132490]Guys, how do I apply AO to my skin?[/QUOTE] I am assuming you have the AO overlay. what i do is i open the skin in GIMP, then open the AO overlay as a layer and open up the layers window. on the layers window there should be a dropdown box at the top that says "normal". Change that to multiply, and then you can play around with the slider under that (i think that slider was called opacity) t change how dark it is. once you are happy with that, right click your AO layer in the layers box and click "merge down". after that, you can hit save or save as to save your skin and you are done!
