Here, I made an inventory hat storage concept. [B]edit[/B] In case you're retarded, yes, I know Notch was joking, sheesh. [img]http://img838.imageshack.us/img838/8940/inventory.png[/img] Okay, here is how it works: The tophat is the leftmost hat, so it is the one that is worn on the viewmodel. It takes up inventory space that cannot be used for anything else, so you have a choice between looking cool and being practical. The Tophat in the image is 3x3 squares, meaning 9 spaces of inventory you can't use unless you drop the hat in a chest or something similar. The green comparison block is what every other item takes up in the inventory, a 1x1 square. You cannot put hats in the head slot because that's where armor goes, and if you could put the hat there you would be vulnerable and without a piece of your armor. If a hat is equipped, head armor can be worn and will have the same effects, but it won't show up on the viewmodel to make room for the hat. Hats can come in all different shapes and sizes, but they all take up inventory space. The two shown here are just a concept.
