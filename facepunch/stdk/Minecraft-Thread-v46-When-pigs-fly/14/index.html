I don't know if this has been posted before, when I woke up there was 225 new posts in the old thread and 11 pages in this thread, so I couldn't be bothered to read them all. A great way to make portals to the nether, is to simply make a form of dirt or cobblestone (Anything easily removed will do.), pour one lava source in the bottom of it. Make water fall on the lava source, obsidian should be created in the place of the lava source. Use this technique to create the whole portal. It works fine in the normal world, but when you're in the nether, the water simply evaporates when you use it. So to create another portal in the nether, you will have to have some mined obsidian to place. Anyway, it is quite fun to make portals this way.
