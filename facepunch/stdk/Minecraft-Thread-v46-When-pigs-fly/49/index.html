[QUOTE=TehSpah;26004260]I can't get any texture packs to work[/QUOTE] You need the pack to be like this. Put it in a similar location to mine here: [img]http://img222.imageshack.us/img222/4473/tmp22l.png[/img] Then you need to make sure when you open the pack, it looks pretty much exactly like this: (Yours may have more or less files.) [img]http://img688.imageshack.us/img688/7078/tmp21.png[/img] Make sure that the GUI, Armor, and Terrain folders as well as the shadow.png and terrain.png are directly inside the zip file. The pack [U][B]MUST[/B][/U] have pack.png and pck.txt. These can be blank files but they must exist for it to work.
Make a .zip on the .minecraft/texturepacks and put the texture pack in that .zip. After that you will be able open up minecraft and open up the texture packs, there should now be your texture packs in there. just click on your texture pack and then press done.
