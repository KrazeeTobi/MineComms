[QUOTE=Appolox;21349703]Craft a rake, after you equip it right click on the grass and rake all the green fields, you get seeds.[/QUOTE] Half-of-the-story much? First of all, it's called a hoe. Combine 2 sticks and 2 wood or stone or iron etc. at a workbench like this: xx- -+- -+- - is empty + is stick x is wood/stone/whatever Get the seeds as quoted. Then, go to a water source and use the hoe to make fields of nearby dirt patches. Wait until the field is hydrated by the water. You can see the dirt change from light brown to dark brown. Take the seeds and put them in the hydrated patches. Make sure sunlight isn't blocked by anything and that no creepers or critters can come close to it. The fields can also be dehydrated and the crops destroyed by walking over them too much, so watch out for that.
