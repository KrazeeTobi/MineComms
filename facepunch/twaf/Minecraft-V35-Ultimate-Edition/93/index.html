For whoever asked about growing grass, you have to have grass blocks touching the dirt blocks or near it to make it grow. So if you have a floating island and need grass on top make a spiral dirt staircase from the ground up to the island. Then put torches all over it to make sure its lit 100% of the time. Then you can either just leave the game running in the background while you're gone or just continue playing in the same chunk and your grass will grow.
