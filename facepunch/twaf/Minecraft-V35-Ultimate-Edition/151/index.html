When the materials near the surface started to dry up, I figured I'd take a plunge and map it all out. That didn't really work out for me. First, I discovered that it was [i]massive[/i]. I started with 64 torches, and right now I believe I have somewhere around 300, lighting the portions of the cave I've explored. I've found several waterfalls, two of which reach the lava near the bottom of the map and a third which swept me into a dead-end cavern where I promptly died from fall damage. When I figured I couldn't explore any more, I decided to work on my own mine... which led me into [i]another[/i] portion of the cave I hadn't yet explored. I thought another opening in the surface a while away was a different cave, until I saw my own torches after exploring some more.
