[QUOTE=hogofwar;24167140]How can i extend the length that my redstone circuit will work for?[/QUOTE] Lots of Repeaters Strings of NOT gates, used in clocks and delays. Using two NOT gates in series can extend your running wire length past the original 15. As of 1.0.2 (the July 6th update), there must be a strip of wire between the two NOT gates. This makes it possible to send long-distance signals to doors, tracks, gates, etc. To reduce delays, you can place one inverter per 15 blocks. If the input is inverted at the other end, just add another inverter.
