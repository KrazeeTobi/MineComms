[QUOTE=Dug;23502061]So I only have a certain amount of time until the price goes up? Could you give me a explanation of what you exactly do in this game?[/QUOTE] Well, Creative is free, so you don't really need an explanation for that one. /game/ is the newest and FINAL paid-only in development version. It has infinite terrain (don't let anyone tell you otherwise), animals, enemies, a day/night cycle, and damn near infinite replayability. You start out punching trees down with your bare hands, and craft blocks into tools like swords and axes and other things like chests and furnaces, where you can smelt ores into usable materials. You can kill pigs for their pork, hit sheep to make their wool fly off, and apparently get milk from cows soon enough. At night, however, the enemies come out, and their only purpose is to completely wreck your shit and murder you. Any enemy that finds you will make a beeline for you and you'll have to either run away or kill it. There are a handful of different enemies, like the exploding creeper or the fierce spider. Basically, you mine and craft, just like the game says. It's a ton of fun and I recommend it to anyone, really. You don't need a superior computer to run it, and it's got really polished graphics. That's my official "sales pitch" but you won't really know for sure unless you try it out. Even if you don't like it, you'll be able to do all the stuff I just described and more with friends when Survival Multiplayer comes out. The price will double when that happens so I assure you it's a better deal to get it now. I can definitely say you won't regret it.
