I stabbed some torches into the shallow walls around me to prepare myself for the night ahead. [img_thumb]http://img101.imageshack.us/img101/4610/96257837.jpg[/img_thumb] Now while it is rather silly for a Dwarf, a creature which spends most of its life underground, to be afraid of the dark, something about this place made the darkness seem foreboding and dangerous.
