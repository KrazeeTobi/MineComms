Okay, here is how the in development portions work. Indev is the first, and least updated version. Notch has said he does not plan to ever update this again, and it is therefore obsolete. Infdev is the second version, the one with infinite terrain (if you say it's not infinite try walking to the end from the spawn point and tell me how that goes). Notch recently stated he was no longer going to update infdev either in favor of the newest version. /Game/ is the newest and "final" version of the in development trio. It is by no means complete, the "final" only implies that this is where the game will launch in the future, that being [url]www.minecraft.net/game/[/url]. Notch will update this version and neither of the others until Survival Multiplayer is released, at which point both previous in development versions will be erased from the site in favor of the main /game/ hub, which will house Creative when it is moved there as well.
