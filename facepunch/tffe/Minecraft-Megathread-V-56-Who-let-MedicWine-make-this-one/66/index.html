Hey, fellows. You all probably know about this, but for the few that don't here's a little list I've compiled of quality Minecraft community stuff. Maybe if someone wants to add a few more entries someone could stick it in the OP of the next thread [URL=http://www.minecrap.com/]Minecrap[/url]-An awesome resource website put together by Bitburner and his 10-year old daughter. Bitburner is an awesome guy who got his two daughters (6 and 10) into playing Minecraft, and he even does a show in which he and his daughters play Minecraft with notable figures in the community. [url=http://www.theshaftpodcast.com/]The Shaft[/url]-By far the best Minecraft podcast out there. Hosted by Brent Copeland, Wes Wilson, and Eric Fullerton of the Dead Workers party network. [url=http://www.planetminecraft.com/]Planet Minecraft[/url]-A great site dedicated to community projects and creations. User created content is uploaded regularly: Skins, Saves, and Texture packs all make their homes here. [url=http://minecraftwb.com/]Minecraft Workbench[/url]- Made by Luclin, MCWB is the most popular resource for Minecraft texture packs, skins, and saves eventually turned into a general community hub that now holds contests and has a forum that rivals the size of the official forum. [url=http://crafthub.net/]Crafthub[/url]-A Minecraft Blog turned into a news-site. Does amazing-awsome stuff like the Idea of the week, popular servers, and even does articles on popular mods. [url=http://minecraftmuseum.net/]Minecraft Museum[/url]-An oft-overlooked site that focuses on allowing users to upload images.It's mostly a gallery, but if you sift through all the crap there's a lot of gems in there. [url=http://www.minefield.fr/]Minefield[/url]-French Minecraft site focusing on large projects and server hosting. I can't understand French, but apparently it's good!
