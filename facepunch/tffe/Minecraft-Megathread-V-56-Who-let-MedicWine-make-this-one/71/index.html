Holy shit, I just found an amazing MP discovery. So I was on a MP server that has had one map for sevreal months, and it had changed spawn location a while back, leaving the old spawn town abandoned. One fine day I decided to ceck out the old spawn town. There was a couple shops, a little town hall, an apartment building, the usual. Most of the houses had gotten greifed,glass windows broken, valuable materials missing, stuff like that. However there was one strange building, a little cobblestone thing, only a couple blocks high and about 3 wide. The floor was made of cobblestone, and it has a small glass showing case mounted above the door that probably held a sign, but the glass had been broken and the sign removed. Walking about 20 blocks ftom the town a started to dig, looking for hidden objects or some ore. Suddenly, I broke into a large, dimly lit cavern, thinking it was a natural cave, I rappeled down with some ladders and found I was in a huge room. I was standing in a large subteranian subway station, that once had gleaming iron walls, but many prices of it had been removed, revealing ugly redstone circuts behind it. Many of the torches had also been removed, so I lit the place up, the subway tunnel looked to be lined with cloth but parts of the cloth had been burned, making sand and gravel flow in and block both tunnels. Sevreal of the rails had been busted up, but there were minecarts scattered about, some derailed. I grabbed a couple sugercane and grilled pork from a derailed crate cart and covered up my hole. The maker of the tunnel was the creator of most of the old spawn and had stopped joining the server weeks ago. :ohdear:
