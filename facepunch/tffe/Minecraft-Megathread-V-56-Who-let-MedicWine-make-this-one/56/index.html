If you chip away the outer layer of Netherrack, you will find a block of pure Thunderbolt Iron in the centre (jet black and shiny), which requires a Diamond Pickaxe to mine, but yields 4 lumps of Thunderbolt Iron, which is pretty much the best material around. Basically, Thunderbolt Iron is twice as durable and effective as Diamond, but is far rarer as it only appears during a rare random encounter. A full set of Thunderbolt Armour is a true medal of honour and dedication, showing that you watch the skies. I also have another new recipe around.
