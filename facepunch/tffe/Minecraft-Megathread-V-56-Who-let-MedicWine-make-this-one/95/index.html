[QUOTE=Oicani Gonzales;27864684]Am I supposed to do something before Nothing changed[/QUOTE] Have the z-rotation selected. The default interpolation is Beizer, so the line is curved so that movements between keyframes are smoother. With Linear, there's no curve at all, just a straight line between two keyframes (which is more ideal for looping animations of spinning things.) The change should be noticeable if you're zoomed in close enough on the line.

Go to the render tab and turn off AA 2. In the materials tab turn off any secularity 3. When you add a texture (Your image) make sure under image sampling make sure you turn off Interpolation 4. Make sure you only have one lamp, otherwise more than one shadow will most likely cast upon the object. If you want a plain render with no shadows at all, go to render tab and under shading turn off shadows.
