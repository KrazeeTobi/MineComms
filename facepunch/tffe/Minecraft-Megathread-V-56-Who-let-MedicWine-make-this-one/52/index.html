Because I'm bored, and PvP is sort of a unknown topic for many, I'm going to make a quick list of tips you should know: 1. [b]SNEAK WHENEVER POSSIBLE.[/b] 90% of the people I encounter never know that sneaking (pressing shift) makes your name tag invisible when behind blocks. It makes you slower, but enables you to hide pretty much anywhere. 2. Always have earphones or speakers on. Without them, you could miss someone tunneling into your base and not be properly prepared. 3. Practice against mobs. Creepers are the perfect enemy to spar against, and get you plenty of sulfur in the process. Do the same with enemies, circling them and using the terrain to your advantage. 4. If possible, choose the battlefield. If you have someone on their tail, lead them into an area you know, so you can hide in places you know of and such. Even better, combat-prepare your bases or locations of hiding by adding nooks and crannies, as well as shortcuts and secret tunnels. 5. Always be prepared. When someone else knows about sneaking, you never know where and when you will be attacked. Always wear armor as well as having your sword in your quickbar. 6. Never hesitate to run from a fight. There will always be someone smarter, stronger, or more prepared than you, and it is better to keep your equipment and run then stay and die against some person with diamond armor and swords. 7. When you lose, keep your cool. [i]Never[/i] yell at someone and call them a "n00b" or any other insult, but congratulate them and keep your cool. You would be respected by many other users and look more mature than you may actually be. 8. Bait. If possible, make a chest filled with iron or other valuables, with the stack spread out amongst the entire chest. It takes a while to grab it all, and most looters are greedy, going for the entire thing. When they are a bit into their grabbing, you can barrage from behind and easily kill them before they can exit out of the chest inventory and run. 9. Know thine enemy. If you know someone to sneak around and kill people, use that against them. Find ways to exploit people's strategies. 10. Never trust anyone in a PvP situation. Unless there is some sort of /party system, anyone can kill you, even friends. 11. Create fear. People will be more nervous fighting you if you have a reputation for sneaking around and playing with your prey. With enough time and effort, you can get anyone to run away from you on sight.
