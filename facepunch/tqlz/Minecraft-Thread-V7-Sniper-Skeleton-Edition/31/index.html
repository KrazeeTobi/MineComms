Well whenever I design a fortress, I always space everything evenly, where I place a dirt marker every four blocks, leaving 3 blocks in between each marker. [img]http://filesmelt.com/dl/C12.PNG[/img] This makes it easy to place evenly spaced fortifications. The blocks that are two pieces high give full cover to anyone behind them. The half blocks make it easy for anyone to get on top of the fortification and look over the edge, while also providing maximum view. [img]http://filesmelt.com/dl/C23.PNG[/img] These fortifications can also be easily converted to window slots, using the half block to provide maximum view, but also ensures no one can fall out of the window. [img]http://filesmelt.com/dl/C33.PNG[/img] Removing the half block gives you a door slot, which if placed high up, gives an archer the ability to lean over the edge and hit any enemies that are next to the wall.
