[b]February 14, 7:00 PM Sheep[/b] Curious as it is, I have found a sheep that will not leave me alone. She follows me around everywhere, regardless of where I go. I even watched her swim to the other side of a river as I crossed. I'm not sure how I know it's a girl, it just makes sense. Tonight when I'd settled in to sleep I heard the sheep outside bleating, and I had no choice but to let her in. It's so lonely out here, perhaps a companion would be good for me. It's just... I can't help but feel overwhelmed. All of this information around me, the sights, the sounds, the life, or, even the lack of life. I can't take it all in. If only there was someone else here, a companion to take notes with me. It's times like this that I miss my wife the most. Ah, but life is so fragile. Today, I plucked a tulip from the ground. It was yellow, beautiful, just budding, in fact. As soon as I'd taken it out of the sunlight, it died. It just withered right in my hands. There was no explaining it. In fact, new flowers sprout every day. As the sun goes down, the plants wither and die, and then they grow anew every day. Hold on, I saw something in the cave, deeper in, where the chest used to be. I will report back in a moment. Staying safe, -J.D.
