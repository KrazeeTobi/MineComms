Hopefully it's fun." *Seasons: Leaves change color, more rain during the spring and fall, colder weather during the winter, causing the upper layer of still water to freeze into ice blocks (only when connected to land or other ice blocks, though, so it'd slowly spread), and snow falling to the ground, creating loads of snow blocks everywhere. *Smoke blocks may be added, necessitating a chimney. *The ability to play as monsters would be possible, he says, and in fact very easy.
