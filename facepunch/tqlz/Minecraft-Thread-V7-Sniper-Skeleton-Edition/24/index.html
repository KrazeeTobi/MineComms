Wow, this game never ceases to amaze me. I've gotta document this, just the cave is crawling with fucking creepers. I've got well over 60 Iron ore, so I'll go stock up on arrows and armour, and perhaps wait till I have full hp. Anyways, I'll describe it for you. I'm about 60 blocks underground. I'm digging a mine tunnel, scouting for ores. I find an iron ore vein dead smack center in my tunnel. I start mining them out, happy with what little resources I can get (at the time, I only had 20 or so iron ore). I dig out some ore on the sides of my tunnel and I get scared. Water starts rushing in, like a great beast devouring my torches. If I don't act fast, I'll end up in pitch black, and perhaps under water. I could die here. I hurriedly seal up the hole in the wall, but something caught my eye just as I closed it up. More resources. Only this time, an abundance of gold! (yes, I know gold is crap, but perhaps in the future it'll be worth something) I carefully break open the wall again, and start draining the pool. This is a natural cave, some sort of travel tunnel. I follow it all the way down, it goes on for about 2-3 minutes. The cave is long! (keep in mind, it's a tunnel, not really a hollowed out cave). I start to lose patience, what if I end up getting lost, all for nothing? Only resources I have found so far was the gold. Just as I am about to turn back, I stumble upon a HUGE cavern. No matter how many torches I place, the ceiling is still pitch black. This place amazes me. Resources are scattered everywhere. I jump up and down with glee, readying myself for the goldrush that is about to happen. Ptew! Twang! I run for cover. There appears to be enemies in this cavern.. I will have to kill them all off.. I easily dispatch the zombies and creepers. It's those damn skeletons that trouble me. They must have been some sort of marksman gods in their past lives. Their aim is true, and I am badly hurt, but my armour saves me. I eventually dispatch of them, but there are many more left. I retreat to a safe distance and decide to explore the other side of the cavern. The cavern floor is dotted with dangerous gaps that go on for god knows how long. I nimbly travel across the floor, avoiding the gaps. Something glowing red catches my eye, I prepare for the worst. Damn spiders. Luckily, however, it is not a spider. It is a cavern BELOW the one i'm in, with magma! God, I can't wait to explore this thing. It's so far from my base, but I'll slowly start excavating speed tunnels to allow quick entry. Thing is, it's so damn huge, that monsters can spawn in dark places I can't reach. Oh well. Will take a long time, but I'll tame this cavern and make it a quarry..
I bought the game.
