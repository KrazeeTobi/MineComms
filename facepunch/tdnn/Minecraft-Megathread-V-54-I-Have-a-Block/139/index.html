Bit of an idea here concerning Glowstone Dust. In this theory, one could place Glowstone Dust on surfaces in a similar fashion to Redstone Dust, and it'd light up an area similar to a Torch. However, if you put a Glowstone Dust in a gap in a Redstone wire, the Glowstone will do something special when the wire is active. Not only does it's luminosity increase, but it will damage nearby undead in a similar fashion to Sunlight, providing a decent anti-undead security system when made in the right fashion. However, having the system on too long will cause the Glowstone to burn out, leaving nothing behind. Same principle would apply to Lanterns if they are made via Glowstone, although a burnout would simply leave an Empty Lantern that would require a Glowstone Dust to repair. The principle of a Glowstone "Fuse" would come in handy for advanced Redstone electrical systems.
