Yo, with the implementation of Sugar, i've gained quite a few potential ideas. First off, combining Sugar, a Bucket of Milk, a Snowball, a Wheat and a Redstone Dust could yield an Icecream, a delicious magical treat capable of rendering you invulnerable to Fire for a minute or two. Another idea would involve the implementation of fruit, in a theoretical event that I title The Fruit Update. This update would implement the chance for Leaf blocks to drop fruit when destroyed, similar to how they drop Saplings at the moment. Some fruits would spawn in multiple biomes, but many of the fruits are native to specific biomes. For example, Apples have a chance to spawn from any Leaf block broken in any biome, but Bananas and Coco Beans only have a spawn-chance in the Rainforest biomes. If you have a Fruit, you have the option of planting it in Farmland, which will yield a Tree with a higher chance of spawning that Fruit from it's leaves, thus encouraging the method of Farming. As I was saying earlier, the Fruit Update would permit a new "placeable bakeable", the Fruit Pie. First, you'd need to craft a Pie Dish, made from a horizontal line of 3 Gold. Then, on the crafting square, place the Pie Dish at the bottom with a Milk Bucket on either side, three Wheat on the top row, and the Fruit of your choice in the centre square (Apple by default, as that's the most common Fruit), with a Sugar on either side. The resulting Fruit Pie would be placeable, like the Cake, and each slice would heal 3 Hearts, but the downside is that the Pie Block only has 4 uses before it disappears, leaving only the Pie Dish. And one more thing; if regular Pigmen ever get implemented, they should be attracted to Apple Pies, willing to do anything to grab a slice, so don't leave the Pie to cool on any open windowsills, or you might just get a visit from "the pig people". Besides, the Pie doesn't really need to cool down anyway. Suggestions?
