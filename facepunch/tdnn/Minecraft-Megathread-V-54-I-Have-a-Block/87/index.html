The benefit of emeralds. The amount of emerald dust (or pure emeralds) used to repair an item is dependent on its "class." Diamonds are class 4. "Hyperdiamonds" (which can only make a pickax, mine everything 1.5 times faster, and has 4048 uses. I have yet to find one.) are the highest (and only) class 5, needing 8 pure emeralds to repair. 1 emerald can be turned into 3 emerald dust.
