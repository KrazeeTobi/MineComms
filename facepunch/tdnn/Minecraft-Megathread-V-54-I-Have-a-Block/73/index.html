They key to any map is to make it fair and balanced, and take into account a few factors. Higher ground always gives someone an advantage, so the higher ground has to be offset by some sort of problem, for instance, difficult-to-navigate areas or monsters to keep people from just fighting on top. [img]http://gyazo.com/08840a92ea3fbe5fb17e31e1a6005096.png[/img] In this map, I tried to offset this by putting lavafalls on each side, which do two things: Give more protection to the people on the bottom, as they can hide behind them, and also limit the area the people on the top can trod. The top is essentially split in two, unless you want to wade through lava. In order to get to the other side, one must either walk across the plank going over the lava (soon to be added) which exposes themselves to arrow fire, or go down a pillar to the bottom to get to the other set of ladders.
