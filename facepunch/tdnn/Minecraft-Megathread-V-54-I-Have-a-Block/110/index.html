[QUOTE=TraderRager;27554378]Make the creek big enough to use boats. :buddy: [editline]21st January 2011[/editline] Also add fishing rods to the loadout.[/QUOTE] I think you are sort of missing the idea of my maps. These maps are focused on deathmatch gameplay, which is pretty much giving people some stuff and throwing them into an arena. I may create a large battlefield at a later date, encouraging long battles and building, but the current model is a very simple battle, with no blocks being created or destroyed. The fishing poles would take too much time to catch fish in the heat of the battle, and someone could easily pick you off with arrows or run up to you and destroy you. Boats would be nice, but just would not be convenient. At this point in SMP, boats aren't really that much faster than people, and are very difficult to handle, making them better for ocean expeditions than tight, riverside combat. That did give me an idea, however: Survival Dome. It's going to be a large biodome, made of adminium, stretching over a large area of naturally generated land. You couldn't dig out, and the only escape would be the glass roof. There would be a river going through the middle, and you could either stage strategic wars that span hours, or faction battles. There would be no cuboid protection, and monsters would spawn. The only difference would be the glass roof, adminium walls stretching to the bottom of the map, and maybe some more diamond ore scattered around under the map, providing faster gameplay. ANOTHER IDEA! In the center of the map, there would be an obsidian capsule with a diamond block or two inside. It would take a diamond pick to pop it open, and it would be in the center of the battlefield, and would provide a lot of fun conflict.
